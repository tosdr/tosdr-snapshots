1. How It Works

Overview. This Application allows you to earn sponsorships on behalf of certain charities (each, a “Charity”) while walking, running, biking, and participating in other activities (each, an “Activity”). You are accessing this Application from an iPhone, Android phone, or, possibly, some other device (each, a “Device”). When you use the Application, we use your Device’s GPS and pedometer functionality to measure the distance of your Activity. For every mile (or portion thereof) that you travel using the Application, you can earn money for a Charity. Here’s how…

Peer-to-Peer Fundraising.  We are grateful to be partnering with the PayPal Giving Fund (“PPGF US“), an IRS-registered 501(c)(3) charity, and PayPal Giving Fund Canada (“PPGF Canada“), a charity registered with the Canada Revenue Agency (No. 812867752RR0001), to enable you to fundraise for any of the charities registered on those respective platforms. PPGF US and PPGF Canada are collectively referred to herein as “PPGF“.  

Please note that just because a charity is listed on PPGF, it does not mean that the charity is one of our partners, or that we have any relationship with them.

However, we are excited that our partnership with PPGF will empower you to fundraise for any of the charities on the PPGF platform, subject to the below terms. 

If you elect to participate in such peer-to-peer fundraising, you shall be able to create your fundraising page (a “Personal Fundraising Page”) through which your friends and family can sponsor you.  (Or you can sponsor yourself.) 

If you or anyone else donates through a Personal Fundraising Page, such donation shall be subject to the following:

(a) For charities that are enrolled in PPGF US, you’re donating to PPGF US, subject to its terms. PPGF US receives the donation, which is typically tax deductible, minus payment processing fees of 1.99% + $0.49. PPGF typically grants funds to recommended charities within 15-45 days. In the rare event PPGF can’t fund your chosen charity, it will (whenever possible) ask you to recommend another, and grant the funds to a similar charity if you don’t respond. 

(b) For charities that are enrolled in PPGF Canada, Your donation, less processing fees of 1.60% + $0.30, is to PPGF Canada, subject to its terms. PPGF typically grants funds to recommended charities within 15-45 days. In the rare event PPGF can’t fund your chosen charity, it will (whenever possible) ask you to recommend another, and reassign the funds to a similar charity if you don’t respond. Your donation may typically be used for a tax credit in Canada, and you will be emailed an official donation receipt issued by PPGF for your records. 

Employee Empowerment Program: The Application has a feature whereby companies can create private teams to sponsor their employees or other invited members.  When a company does this, it sets its sponsorship terms, including the benefitting charity, total budget, and rate per mile.

Charity Sponsors: The Application enables our partner charities to feature their publicly-facing corporate partners. Likewise, companies may, from time to time, sponsor specific charities in the Application.  In these cases, the sponsorship terms (including the budget and rate per mile) shall be agreed upon by the company and the charity.

Cheating. The Application has fairly sophisticated algorithms to detect cheating. So, if you cheat, it will probably be obvious to us and we reserve the right to disqualify any session that appears suspicious, with or without notice to you. Of course, if you’re determined to cheat, then we probably can’t stop you. But if that’s the case, then you’ll just have to live with yourself as a cheater. You’re on the honor system here. So, please don’t cheat.
