How We Collect Information from our Website and the Internet

We collect personal information (such as your name, street address, email address and phone number) that you have voluntarily submitted to us through features on our website such as contact forms. We may also temporarily log your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, administer our website, and track usage statistics. Your IP address may vary each time you visit, or it may be the same, depending on your internet provider. Either way, we cannot identify you through your IP address, and we make no attempt to do so. If you reached our website by clicking on a link or advertisement on another website, then we also track that information. This helps us maximize our internet exposure and understand our users' interests. All this information is collected and used only in the aggregate; that is, it is entered into our database, where we can use it to generate overall reports on our visitors, but not individual reports that identify you personally. We also may retain copies of your written communications with Toll Brothers, which may contain personal information, regardless of the medium through which you correspond with Toll Brothers, including, but not limited to communications the Company receives from you through the website, internet or other electronic or paper means.

We also use "cookies," which are small pieces of data (text files) that a website — when visited by a user — asks the user's browser to store on your computer, mobile device, or any other device through which you access a website. A cookie may contain information that allows us to track your path through our website and to determine whether you have visited us before. A cookie contains no information that would allow us to identify you. We use cookies to help us understand how visitors use our website and help us improve our website. You may refuse to accept a cookie from us by following the procedures specific to your web browser; however, this may affect our ability to provide you with a full user experience. By default, cookies will be stored on your device, unless you opt out of the use of cookies through your browser settings or by clicking on the Cookie Preferences link at the bottom of the Toll Brothers website. We use web beacons on our website or other websites, and sometimes permit third parties to place them on our website to monitor the effectiveness of advertising or for other legitimate purposes. A web beacon is a small, graphic image on a webpage, web-based document or in an e-mail message that is designed to allow the website owner or a third party to monitor who is visiting a website. Web beacons are often invisible to the user because they are typically very small (only 1-by-1 pixel) and the same color as the background of the webpage, document, or e-mail message. Web beacons are represented as HTML IMG tags in the webpage; users can click on "view profiles" of the webpage to see whether the page is using a web beacon.
