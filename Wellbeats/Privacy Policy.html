Privacy Policy

How we use your data:

  • We will use your profile data and class play data to make personalized class and program recommendations to you and improve the product experience.
  • We will not give, rent or sell your data to third parties. We may share limited pieces of your information to our carefully selected partners who help us provide our products or services to you. They are required to have the same security controls and are strictly prohibited from sharing, renting or selling any of your information that we have shared with them.
  • We may share your class play minutes, number of class plays, and class titles with your sponsor/employer but only when they have a legally valid and reasonable business need such as paying you incentives for participation.
  • If you wish to have your data deleted or removed from our system at any time you can request to do so. Please be aware that the removal or deletion of data will result in a deletion of your account and the inability to use the product until you decide to create a new account.
  • We take data privacy and security seriously and are continually taking active measures to ensure the security of our platform. In the event of a data breach, you will be notified of the incident.

LifeSpeak Inc. (“LifeSpeak”, “we”, “us”, “our”, including all LifeSpeak Entities, as defined below), are committed to protecting and using your Personal Information (as defined below) responsibly. We are guided and governed by applicable federal, provincial, and state privacy legislation.

This privacy policy describes how LifeSpeak collects, uses, and discloses your Personal Information. It applies to all Personal Information that you provide to us, including on the websites of LifeSpeak Entities (collectively, the “Websites”), and on applications provided by LifeSpeak Entities (collectively, the “Apps”).

Please read this policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personal Information.

Summary

  • Use of your Personal Information: We use your data to provide you with our products and services, as well as for limited internal purposes described in this policy (e.g., to improve our services). We will also use your profile class play data to make personalized class and program recommendations to you, should you opt into this service.
  • Sharing your Personal Information with your Employer/Sponsor: In limited cases, we share certain Personal Information with your employer/sponsor, such as for the purpose of verifying whether you are eligible for an incentive payment. We do not share more information than is required, and in most cases, this information is limited to your name, class play minutes and number of class plays.
  • Access to your Personal Information by Third Parties: In limited cases, we share some of your Personal Information with our service providers for the purposes of providing our products and services to you. They can only use that information to provide you with our products and services. We require that these service providers have substantially similar controls in place to protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.
  • Security of your Personal Information: We take the privacy and data security very seriously. We improve our systems continually to improve the security of our systems.
  • Deletion of your Personal Information: You can submit a request for us to delete your Personal Information from our systems at any time by submitting a request at member.support@lifespeak.com. Please be aware that the deletion of your Personal Information from our systems will result in the deletion of your user account, and you will not be able to use our products or services until you create a new account.

  • Internet or other electronic network activity information, such as browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, pages viewed on our site.

Your professional(s) may record Personal Information in our systems, such as their interactions with you, their evaluations, records and notes and other information related to your health and/or health care.

Using personal information

We use your Personal Information to:

  • Deliver our products and services to you, including customer support and to deliver personalized class and program recommendations;
  • Communicate with you;
  • De-identify and/or anonymize your Personal Information;
  • Understand how you access and utilize features and services on our Websites and in our Apps in order to troubleshoot, enhance, improve and optimize our products and services;
  • Carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts (including our Terms of Service, with you or to comply with our legal obligations;
  • Screen for potential patterns of misuse and/or fraudulent use of our systems;
  • Estimate our audience size and usage patterns and analyze trends and track movements around our Websites and our systems;
  • Gather demographic information about the users of our Websites and our systems;
  • Assess the performance of our staff for continuing quality improvement;
  • Fulfill any other purpose with your consent.

Please note that we may also use your personal information for research and development purposes, in which case information is de-identified and aggregated, meaning it does not personally identify you. We may also share de-identified or anonymized data with your employer/sponsor.

Specifically, we require our service providers and data processors to have appropriate security procedures and controls in place to protect Personal Information in a manner that is consistent with our Privacy Policy and the security practices and regulations applicable in their country of jurisdiction.

When we store your Personal Information or engage a service provider, they may operate in a jurisdiction that is different than the one in which you are located, and thus your Personal Information may be stored, processed, accessed, or used in another country. In that case, your Personal Information may be subject to the law of the jurisdiction in which it is used or stored, including any law permitting or requiring the disclosure of the information to a government, government agency, court, or law enforcement agency of that jurisdiction. LifeSpeak stores data on servers in both Canada and the United States. The countries in which our service providers operate are in both Canada and the United States.

For Quebec residents: Please note that your information may be transferred outside of Quebec.

LifeSpeak may also share your Personal Information where we believe, upon reasonable grounds, that it is necessary to protect the rights, privacy, safety or property of an identifiable person or group, including exchanging information with other companies and organizations for the purposes of fraud protection. This includes disclosing your Personal Information if necessary to protect against a clear and imminent risk of serious harm to any individual.  For example, LifeSpeak may share your Personal Information if:

  1. You appear to be impaired as the result of alcohol or drugs, and then attempt to operate a motor vehicle, go to a safety-sensitive job, or take any other action in which such impairment could put yourself or others at risk of significant harm. In such cases, we may also warn law enforcement authorities and/or your employer or professional regulator, that you may be at risk of putting yourself and/or others in danger.
  2. LifeSpeak believes that you are at imminent risk of suicide;
  3. LifeSpeak believes that you are at risk of committing homicide.
  4. Disclosure is necessary to protect an identifiable child, consistent with applicable law.
  5. Disclosure is necessary to protect a vulnerable adult who by virtue of condition or circumstance is unable to seek support or assistance for abuse or neglect, consistent with applicable law.

Once no longer required, your Personal Information will be securely destroyed or anonymized (so the information no longer identifies you).
