PRIVACY STATEMENT

Your privacy is important to LinangData. This privacy statement provides information about the Personal Information that LinangData collects, and the ways in which LinangData uses that Personal Information.

Personal Information collection

LinangData may collect and use Personal Information about your use of this website, namely information about browsing and linking ("Personal Information");

The Tools available on LinangData, namely, formatters, validators, encoders & decoders, code beautifiers, generators, converters, string utilities, and other web resources available on linangdata.com (the "Tools") are primarily client-side tools and do not store data on a server.

Where a tool uses a third-party API, LinangData does not make any representation or guarantee how this data is used by the third-party API. For example, the "Grammar & Style" tool, is processed by a third-party API provided by https://languagetool.org.

Using Personal Information

LinangData may use your Personal Information to administer this website; enable your access to and use of the website services; and improve the services it offers and monitor the traffic and Tools usage;

We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device using mechanisms such as cookies, browser web storage (including HTML 5) and application data caches.

Some of our business partners may use cookies on our site (for example, advertisers, Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Facebook Social ("Third Party Add-Ons")). LinangData does not track Sensitive Information or single users. We at LinangData use Google Analytics to collect data. We need this data to understand how you use our website so we can improve its design and functionality. We also need the data to get the most out of our marketing campaigns.

We may collect information about your computer, including IP addresses, OS and browser type for system administration and to report general information to our advertisers. This data is statistical about browsing actions and patterns and does not identify any individual user.

We may obtain information about general internet usage by using a cookie file which is stored on the hard drive of your computer. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive. Cookies help us to improve our site and to deliver a better and more personalised service. They allow us to estimate audience size and usage, remember your preferences and recently used features when you return to our site, and allow us to customize our site according to your individual interests.

You can refuse to accept cookies by adjusting your browser to refuse setting of cookies. If you do this you may be unable to access certain parts of our site.

No data or file uploaded to LinangData's servers is read, analysed or processed.
