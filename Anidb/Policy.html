AniDB Policies: General Terms of Service (TOS) and Privacy Policy

Introduction


These General Terms of Service (TOS) and Privacy Policy (collectively, our "Policy" or "Policies") govern the conditions for using the anidb.net Websites ("Website", "Site"), and the manner in which AniDB ("We", "Us", "Our") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from Users (each a "User", or collectively as "Users") of the Website. By continuing to use AniDB, you acknowledge that you accept our Policies, which may be updated from time to time. AniDB reserves the right to update its Policies at any time, without notice.


AniDB stands for "Anime DataBase". We are a not-for-profit organization that features detailed information about all forms of CJK (Chinese, Japanese, and Korean) animation. We also offer a public forum for our users to discuss anime.

This Policy document serves as our General Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, and is not an exhaustive list. AniDB reserves the right to moderate the use of our services as we deem appropriate and necessary.


To solve some common misconceptions about AniDB:


  • AniDB website and AniDB's IRC channels do not host any files for download, and they do not link to any files for download, notwithstanding the following exception:
    • From time to time, we may host client software for Users to download, such as an "API client", which allow the User to interact with AniDB without the use of a web browser.
  • Offering to share files or direct links to files, in the forum or on our IRC channels, will generally get you banned, as will requesting other Users to provide such files to you. Our staff have full discretion in applying and moderating these rules.
    • Our Forum Rules can be found here: https://anidb.net/forum/thread/10096
  • Please refer to the Policy section below, entitled Third-Party Websites and Content, in cases where AniDB provides a link to external websites.
  • Please refer to the Policy section below, entitled Contacting Us, should you need to contact AniDB.
  • AniDB is a public anime database; anyone can register as a User, and add new information to the database or modify existing information in the database. In general, database entries are not verified or vetted by AniDB staff members before they are inserted into the DB. As such, AniDB expressly cannot guarantee the validity of any entries in our database, and AniDB cannot be held responsible for the content of the database entries. While AniDB does not guarantee the validity of entries in our database, we do periodically undertake a "best-effort" random review of database entries. Should you see an error in any of the database entries on AniDB, you may submit a change request to make an edit.

We reserve the right to block a User from accessing portions of our Website, up to and including the entire Website, at any time and without notice, for violating our General Terms of Service or for any other reason, as we deem appropriate.

What you may do: You can access AniDB data via all of our supported channels, including:

    This list is non-exhaustive.


What you may NOT do: You cannot scrape the HTML page, the database, or attempt to make a copy of the database:

  • You cannot abuse the export tool.

    This list is non-exhaustive.

Use AniDB via our website (for humans only!) or via our API (for bots and scripts). Don't flood the API, scrape the API, or scrape the website. When in doubt, please Contact Us on IRC.

Removal of Your Account


As required by Article 17 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), AniDB will remove your account if you so desire. Using the email address associated with your AniDB account, please send us an email addressed to info@anidb.net and state that you want your account removed. Please note that if there are multiple accounts associated with that email address, we will remove all related accounts unless you specify otherwise.


Please note that the process is not instant; as per the Regulations under the GDPR, AniDB has up to 30 days to comply with your request.


Furthermore, please note that AniDB will not remove data which AniDB does not consider personal and is used to enrich AniDB. Such "enriching" content includes, but is not limited to: reviews, recommendations, similar anime suggestions, votes, and forum posts; all of these items are available publicly on the Site . AniDB considers forum posts as public content since the forums can be accessed without restriction; we will not remove forum posts as part of an account removal request. If you wish, you can manually review your forums posts and remove any personal data from them; however, to reduce disruption to the discussion in the affected forum thread, we ask that you be considerate and minimise any modifications or deletions.


  • AniDB will pseudonymize the username associated with "enriching" content. This is an alternative to outright deletion, and it is a valid method for AniDB to comply with your request under the GDPR Regulations. We will gladly arbiter your appeal; however, AniDB reminds you that to ensure the continued operation of the Site, it is reasonable that AniDB retain rudimentary pseudonymized non-personal data associated with content contributed to AniDB.

If you have questions about the Account Removal procedure, contact us via IRC.
