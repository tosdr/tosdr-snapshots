    MillerKnoll has implemented reasonable technical and organizational measures to maintain physical, technical, and procedural safeguards that are appropriate to the sensitivity of the applicable Personal Information. These safeguards are designed to protect your Personal Information from loss and unauthorized access, copying, use, modification, or disclosure. We strongly advise you not to communicate any confidential information through email to us or otherwise through our Site.

  12. Cookies and Interest Based Advertising

    We use Cookies to receive and store certain types of information whenever you interact with our Site through your computer or mobile device. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access most websites on the Internet or open certain emails. Cookies allow a website to remember information about you, such as your user preferences, unique identifiers, and other information. More specifically, we use Cookies to personalize your experience when you visit our Site, uniquely identify your computer for security purposes and enable us and our third party service providers to serve ads on our behalf across the Internet.

    We and other third parties, engage in interest-based advertising to deliver advertisements and personalized content that we and other advertisers believe will be of interest to you. Third parties will be responsible for their own use of Personal Information collected through the Cookies.

    Advertisements may be delivered to you based on your online or mobile behavior (on our Site and on third party websites), your search activity, your responses to one of our advertisements or communications, the pages you visit, your general geographic location, or other information. These ads may appear on our Site or on third party websites. The third parties with whom we work to help us conduct interest-based advertising may be members of self-regulatory associations such as the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) and the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA). For any Site directed at persons located in the European Union, we may work with third parties who are members of the European Digital Advertising Alliance (eDAA). Visit the links provided to exercise choice with respect to interest-based advertising or click on the AboutAds icon on a MillerKnoll advertisement and follow the instructions on how to opt-out.

    If you change computers, devices, or browsers; use multiple computers, devices, or browsers; or delete your Cookies, you may need to repeat this process for each computer, device, or browser. Opting-out of interest-based advertising will not opt you out of all advertising, but rather only interest-based advertising from MillerKnoll or its agents or representatives.

    You may also see advertisements for third parties on our Site or other websites or properties, based on your visits to, and activities on, our Site and other websites or properties.

    Visit each MillerKnoll brand website to view the respective Cookie List in their Cookie Notice and learn more about how to update your Cookie settings.

  13. Your Privacy Rights

    You may exercise your rights via the Privacy Rights Portal.

    13.1 Right to Opt-Out of Marketing or Withdraw Consent

    In case we ask for your consent to process your Personal Information, you are free to refuse to give it. If you have given your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without any adverse consequences. The lawfulness of any Processing of your Personal Information that occurred prior to the withdrawal of your consent will not be affected.

    Promotional Emails. You may opt-out of receiving certain future email communications from us by clicking on the unsubscribe link at the bottom of emails you receive from us. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to process such requests in a timely manner. You cannot opt-out of receiving transactional emails or communications related to your account with us.

    Promotional Mailings. You may opt-out of physical mail marketing and catalogs by completing the catalog opt-out form on each of the MillerKnoll brand websites. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to process such requests in a timely manner.

    Promotional Text Messages. If you receive a text message from us that contains promotional information you can opt-out of receiving future text messages by replying “STOP.”

    You may also opt-out of marketing via our Privacy Rights Portal.

    13.2 Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing

    We may disclose the Personal Information identified in Section 3 above to third parties for their targeted marketing purposes and to enhance your experience on our Site. All customers have the right to opt-out of the sale of their Personal Information by undertaking both of the following steps:

    1. Submit an Opt-Out of Sales/Sharing/Targeted Advertising request through on our webform; and
    2. Disable the use of online activity tracking and data sharing through Cookies, you can opt-out of each Cookie category (except strictly necessary Cookies) by clicking on the “Cookie Settings” button in the Cookie Notice found at the bottom of each MillerKnoll brand website. You must complete this step on each of our MillerKnoll brand’s websites from each browser and on each device that you use. These steps are necessary so that we can place a first party Cookie signaling that you have opted-out on each browser and each device you use.

    Please note the following:

    1. If you block Cookies, we will be unable to comply with your request to Opt-Out of Sales/Sharing/Targeted Advertising with respect to device data that we automatically collect and disclose to third parties online using Cookies.
    2. If you clear Cookies, you will need to disable the use of Cookies in the preference center again on each browser on each device where you have cleared Cookies.

    To the extent required by law, we will honor opt-out preference signals sent in a format commonly used and recognized by businesses, such as an HTTP header field or JavaScript object. We will process opt-out preference signals at the browser level.

    13.3 Right to Deletion

    You have the right to request that we delete Personal Information about you that we have collected from you, but you should note that in many situations we must keep your Personal Information to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or for another one of our business purposes. You may exercise your rights via the Privacy Rights Portal.

    13.4 Right to Access/Know and Portability

    You have the right to request that we disclose certain Personal Information that we have about you. You also have the right to obtain a copy of your Personal Information in a readily usable format. You may exercise your rights via the Privacy Rights Portal.

    13.5 Right to Correction

    You have the right to make a request to correct or modify inaccurate Personal Information that we have about you. You can access and update most of your account information on our Site by logging into your account. You may also exercise your rights via the Privacy Rights Portal.

    13.6 Right to Restrict Use

    In certain circumstances, you may have the right to restrict the use of your Personal Information or object to the automated Processing activities that are used to evaluate characteristics about you. You may exercise your rights via the Privacy Rights Portal.

    13.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint

    You have the right to lodge a complaint with an authority in the state or country in which you reside or where the conduct that is the subject of the complaint occurred.

    13.8 Verification

    We may seek certain Personal Information to verify your identity that may include email address, mailing address, and government issued identification. We may use third party verification companies to help us verify your identity. If you are submitting a request on behalf of a household, we will need to verify each member of the household in the manner set forth in this Section.

    13.9 Use of Authorized Agent

    If you are making any of the requests above through an authorized agent, we will request written authorization from you and will seek to verify your identity in the manners stated above (depending on the request type), or we will accept a legal Power of Attorney to the authorized agent. To make a request using an authorized agent contact privacy@millerknoll.com.

    13.10 Timing of Response

    We will process Opt-Out of Sales/Sharing/Targeted Advertising requests within fifteen (15) calendar days. We will respond all other requests within thirty (30) calendar days, unless we need more time in which case, we will notify you and may take up to ninety (90) calendar days total to respond to your request.

    If we deny your rights request, you may have the right to appeal. To submit an appeal, submit your request via privacy@millerknoll.com. We will inform you in writing of our response to your appeal.
