Protecting Data Subject privacy and maintaining the integrity of Personal Data means that: The Controller will keep Personal Data and the business between the Data Subject and the Controller in confidence; The Controller will not sell, rent or loan a Data Subjects information to third parties; The Controller will respect a Data Subjects privacy when the Controller may contact a Data Subject concerning various products and services which the Controller make available to a Data Subject from time to time; The Controller may have control over who obtains, uses and the circumstances to give out information about a Data Subject; A Data Subject will have access to the information that the Controller has about the Data Subject.

Scope
This Policy Privacy applies to the Controllers products, services and websites, except where otherwise noted and does not concern any websites that a Data Subject may visit by external links.
