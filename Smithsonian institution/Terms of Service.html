Stay on topic. The Smithsonian and its audiences expect comments to deal with the topic set in the original post, article, or other piece of content.  

The goal is to learn. Express yourself in ways that are befitting of an educational environment for persons of all ages. Post nothing threatening, defamatory, obscene, or sexually explicit here. Do not post profanity or repetitive or very similar messages.  

No spamming, selling, or advertising. This is not the place to promote non-Smithsonian products, services, causes, or candidates for office.

Stay lawful. Post only what is yours to post—do not post any intellectual property that you do not have the rights to post. Do not spread malicious or harmful software or other technology. Do not suggest or encourage illegal activity. 

Take personal responsibility. These are public sites. Please do not post your or anyone else’s personal or other information you would not want the public to know, and again, do not post what is not yours to post.
