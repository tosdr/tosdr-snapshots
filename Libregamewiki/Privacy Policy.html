Libregamewiki:Privacy policy

From Libregamewiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A complete history of edits is kept and publically viewable. It includes the account name of the author (ip-address if not logged in), the timestamp and the changed text.

The web server logs access and errors. These logs are used to analyse error situations and fight SPAM and similar abuse.
