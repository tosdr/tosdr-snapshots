  1. PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect Personal Information and Personal Data as defined under applicable privacy laws (collectively, “Personal Information) when you create an account or communicate with us.  We also collect certain persistent identifiers automatically when you use the Services. Personal Information does not include information that we de-identify or aggregate consistent with applicable law. 

Personal Information We Collect Directly From You

  • Contact Information. We may collect your name, email and related general contact information, including login and password information, when you set up an account with us.
  • Communications Information. When you email, call, or otherwise communicate with us and with members of our team, we may collect and maintain a record of your contact details, communications, and our responses. We also maintain records of communications and information that you post on our social media channels and information you provide to us related to any customer support requests.
  • Financial Information. When payments are required, we use third party payment processors to conduct payments in connection with the Services.

Personal Information We Collect Automatically Through the Services

When you use our Services, we collect: 

  • Web server log information that web browsers and servers make available, including:
    • Browser type;
    • Language preference;
    • Date and time of each visitor request;
    • URLs;
    • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, but note that we permanently delete the last portion of the IP address and hash the full IP address before storing it, thus preventing us from having the ability to use it to transmit any information to or otherwise identify your device;
  • Data about your device, such as your screen resolution, operating system, device manufacturer and model, and language.
  • Browsing activity and information regarding websites and content accessed such as ads displayed on sites you visit.
  • Data about your use of the App, including content viewed, features used and the dates and times of your interactions with the App.

     We use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you as you use or return to the Services so that we can understand how the Services are used, optimize user engagement and customize and enhance your experience.   

Personal Information Collected from Third Parties

We may also collect and receive certain Personal Information about you from third party sources, such as from affiliates, business partners operating systems and social media platforms, public databases, and service providers who provide services or perform functions on our behalf. In addition, we may collect the following Personal Information from third-party sources:

  • Integrated Service Information. We may make available the ability for you to access, register for, sign up, or otherwise interact, engage, or connect with the Services through certain third-party features or integrated services. By accessing our Services via these third-party connections or integrated services, such as through your Google, Facebook, or Apple accounts, we may collect or obtain certain Personal Information about you related to your use of such third-party services. For example, we may collect information related to your account and profile, such as your name, username, email, profile picture, friend lists, public postings, and other similar information available on your profile. You should check your privacy settings on each integrated service to better understand how your Personal Information may be used, disclosed, or otherwise processed by these parties.

  1. COOKIES, PIXELS, AND OTHER TECHNOLOGIES

Our Website uses monitoring technologies including: 

  • Cookies. A cookie is a piece of information that a website stores on a users computer, and that the users browser provides to the website each time the user submits a query to the website using that browser. When you access our Website, we place cookies on your computer. Some cookies allow us to make it easier for you to navigate our Sites, while others are used to enable a faster log-in process or to allow us to track your activities on our Sites. There are two types of cookies: session and persistent cookies.
  • Pixel Tags. Pixel tags (sometime called web beacons or clear GIFs) are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies. While cookies are stored locally on your device, pixel tags are embedded invisibly within web pages and online content. We may use these, in connection with our Services to, among other things, track the activities of users and help us manage content and compile usage statistics.
