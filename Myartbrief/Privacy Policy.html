Privacy Policy

This privacy policy notice is for this website, www.myartbrief.com and served by MyArtBrief Ltd and governs the privacy of those who use it. The purpose of this policy is to explain to you how we control, process, handle and protect your personal information while browsing or using this website, including your rights under current laws and regulations. If you do not agree to the following policy you may wish to cease viewing and using this website.

Key definitions

  • "I", "our", "us", or "we" refer to the business, MyArtBrief Ltd.
  • "you", "the user" refer to the person(s) using this website.
  • "GDPR" means General Data Protection Act.
  • "PECR" means Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulation.
  • "ICO" means Information Commissioner's Office.
  • "Cookies" mean small files stored on a users computer or device.

Processing of your personal data

We are registered with the ICO under the Data Protection Register and our registration number is ZA147598.

Under the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) we control and process any personal information you provide to us electronically for the purpose of facilitating art commissions and sales defined in our 'User Agreement' and 'Artist Agency Agreement'.

We use the contract lawful basis under GDPR for the processing of your information to facilitate the art commission or sale service between a customer and artist. We shall continue to process your information until the contract between us ends or is terminated under any contract terms. Your personal data under the contract lawful basis is not shared with any parties other than to facilitate the service we provide.

You can manage your personal information at any time through our online system. We will continue to hold and process your information until you withdraw consent via email to info@myartbrief.com at which point we will remove all your personal data from our system within 7 working days.

For Artists, we use the consent lawful basis under GDPR for processing of your name and email address so that you can participate in any partner schemes from third party companies we have agreements with. We will not pass this information to any third parties without your consent.

If, as determined by us, the lawful basis upon which we process your personal information changes, we will notify you about the change and any new lawful basis to be used if required. We shall stop processing your personal information if the lawful basis used is no longer relevant.

Your Individual Rights

Under the GDPR your rights are as follows. You can read more about your rights in details here;

  • the right to be informed;
  • the right of access;
  • the right to rectification;
  • the right to erasure;
  • the right to restrict processing;
  • the right to data portability;
  • the right to object; and
  • the right not to be subject to automated decision-making including profiling.

You also have the right to complain to the ICO www.ico.org.uk if you feel there is a problem with the way we are handling your data. We handle subject access requests in accordance with the GDPR.

Internet cookies

We use cookies on this website to provide you with a better user experience.

We do this by placing a small text file on your device / computer hard drive to track how you use the website, to record or log whether you have seen particular messages that we display, to keep you logged into the website where applicable.

Some cookies are required to enjoy and use the full functionality of this website.

We use a cookie control system which allows you to accept the use of cookies, and control which cookies are saved to your device / computer. Some cookies will be saved for specific time periods, where others may last indefinitely. Your web browser should provide you with the controls to manage and delete cookies from your device, please see your web browser options.

Cookies that we use are defined in Schedules 1, 1A and 2 at the end of this policy.

Data security and protection

We ensure the security of any personal information we hold by using secure data storage technologies and precise procedures in how we store, access and manage that information.

Our methods meet the GDPR compliance requirement. Personal information in our cloud database is encrypted and communication with our website is protected by an SSL certificate.

Transparent Privacy Explanations

We have provided some further explanations about user privacy and the way we use this website to help promote a transparent and honest user privacy methodology.

Adverts, Sponsored links and Affiliate Tracking

Our website does not contain adverts, sponsored or affiliate links on any page.

About our Service

MyArtBrief is the home of UK commission artists who love and thrive on the collaboration process in creating artwork personal to you. You can also buy art online.

It is the only service that allows you to find artists and facilitate art commissions entirely online so if you are looking to commission a painting, commission art or commission an artist, you came to the right place!

View our How It Works section which has a step by step video guide to our art commissions process. If you have any questions, try our FAQ page. If you still have a query you can contact us via email and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
