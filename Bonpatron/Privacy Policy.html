Nadaclair Language Technologies Privacy Notice

Who is collecting the data?

The data is collected by Nadaclair Language Technologies Inc. through the following websites: BonPatron.com, SpellCheckPlus.com, SpanishChecker.com and ItalianChecker.com (and associated subdomains such as pro.BonPatron.com).

What data is being collected?

We don't store texts, though subscribers have the option of maintaining a personal text archive. User accounts (for subscribers) have the information from their profile, such as email address and email options, as well as account creation and expiry dates. For all users standard web log information is stored (time, request, IP address) for the purposes of monitoring server performance and use. Cookies are used to store the username (email address) and preferences (such as language proficiency). We also use Google Analytics to better understand how the sites are being used. We do not store any financial information on our servers from subscribers.

What is the legal basis for processing the data?

Users willingly submit texts for grammatical analysis.

Will the data be shared with any third parties?

None of the data collected will be shared with any third party.

How will the information be used?

We may use contact information to make users aware of developments.

How long will the data be stored for?

We don't store texts, though subscribers have the option of maintaining a text archives. For the free sites, texts are submitted anonymously and we have no way of identifying individual users or their texts.

What rights does the data subject have?

The data subject has the right to anonymity and to the privacy of their contact information.

How can the data subject raise a complaint?

The data subject can raise a complaint with us directly at mail@nadaclair.com
