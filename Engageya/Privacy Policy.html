We briefly explain herein about how cookie collection work, and designed a Cookie Policy to further explain about the type of cookies we use, how and why.

      • Cookies

        A cookie is a small text file that is placed on the browser of the hard drive of your computer (or similar device) by websites that you visit. Most websites and online services use cookies. Cookies are typically used together with web beacons in order to make websites or web services function, or function more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the operator of the particular website or service. Cookies make your use of the Services easier to use and improve their functionality. In some of the Platforms or Serviceswe operate, Cookies are essential for the proper functionality of that Platform or Services. Most browsers are automatically set to accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to disable cookies or to remove them. If you choose to disable cookies though you will still be able to access the Website and use the Services, but some features and services may not properly function.

      • Web Beacons

        We and our advertising partners may use standard Internet technology such as web beacons (or clear gifs), tracking pixels, and similar technologies to gather analytics and anonymous, non- identifiable information about End Users that interact with the Services or content therein. This is usually used to better understand Users’ likes and dislikes to better improve a content service engines and technologies.

      • Log Files

        Technical information from your browser is automatically collected into a log file whenever you visit our Site or interact with any of the Services. This information includes your device’s IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, the content of any undeleted cookies that the End User’s browser previously accepted from us, and the referring website address. For more information about cookies and our use of them on the Site and inthe course of our Services, refer to our Cookie Policy
