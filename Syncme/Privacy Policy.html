INTRODUCTION

Sync.Me Technologies Ltd. (“Sync.Me”, “our”, “we”, “us” or the “Company”) is committed to respect the privacy and Personal Information of the users (“user” or “you”) in connection with the use of our mobile application and/or by accessing our website https://sync.me/ (collectively the “Service”).

  • Our Service is primarily designed to empower consumers to make informed decisions and deter illegal and fraudulent calling activities.

  • Our Service plays a key role in enhancing connectivity and thwarting intrusive and fraudulent calls, by introducing a variety of smart features that empower consumers to make informed decisions and reduce the risks of the user community from being targeted by malicious or misleading activities. These features include:

    • Reporting and blocking spam: our users can choose to report and block numbers to be added to their personal spam call list, and potentially a regional spam call list. In the event a particular number was reported as spam by a certain number of users, other users will be notified if they receive a call from that same number.

    • Live Caller ID and Spam detection: our Live Caller ID displays the name of the caller before the consumer answers the call, and flags if the number was reported as spam.

    • Number search (reverse lookup): individuals, including those who are not registered users with Sync.ME, may manually search a number on Sync.ME's web-based platform in order to ascertain the identity of the caller and whether a number was reported as spam. The contact information collected by Sync.ME is used solely to empower individuals to identify incoming calls from unknown sources, identify unknown numbers through the use of reverse numeric lookup, as well as to report and block fraudulent, intrusive or inappropriate calls.

  • We designed our features to better respond to deceptive and abusive practices aided by anonymity, both in telemarketing as well as in other abusive or deceptive practices (such as abusive calls).

  • Our goal is to promote consumer privacy by allowing them to know who is calling them, as well as to promote accountability throughout the industry.

  • Important note: Nothing in this Privacy Policy is intended to limit in any way your statutory rights, including your rights to a remedy or means of enforcement.

  • How to withdraw consent? If you would like to remove your Personal Information from our Service, you may do so by visiting this link: https://sync.me/unsubscribe.

  • Please read our CCPA privacy policy here [https://sync.me/privacy-ccpa]
