Cookie Notice

This Cookie Notice applies to the use by Blocksi of Cookies and other similar technologies on the Blocksi website www.blocksi.net, (“Website”). The purpose of this notice is to provide you with the information about what Cookies are and how they work. It also explains what Cookies we use, what they do, and how you can control what Cookies we use when you visit our Website. This notice should be read alongside our Website Privacy Policy. The Website Privacy Policy provides full details of how and why we use your personal information when you are using this Website and provides details about your legal rights in relation to your personal information and how to contact us.

What Is a Cookie

A Cookie is a small data file sent from a server to your web browser. They are stored in your browser’s cache and allow a website or a third party to recognise your browser. Cookies can be useful because they allow a website to recognize your device, preferences, and generally help to improve your online experience.

Which Cookies Do We Use?

This Website uses different types of Cookies; some are placed by third party services that appear on our pages.

When you visit our Website, we ask for your permission to use Cookies; that can be changed or withdrawn at any time. The law states that we can store Cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this Website. For all other types of Cookies we need your permission. See the list of all the Cookies we use in this Section.

Your consent applies to the following domain:
Blocksi.net

If at any time you want to change your Cookie settings, including withdrawing your consent to this processing, we would encourage you to do this via the links below. You may need to refresh the page before the changes to the settings take effect.

Session Cookies, Temporary Cookies, and First- and Third-Party Cookies

Session Cookies are deleted automatically from your device when you close your web browser. Cookies that have a specific duration will remain on your device until that term expires, unless you manually delete them before the expiry date.

We place some of the Cookies on your device ourselves (first-party Cookies), while other Cookies are placed by third parties (third-party Cookies). See the column “Provider” in the Cookie tables above for specific information per Cookie. Third-party Cookies belong to and are managed by other parties, and may collect data outside of our Website as well.

Enabling, Disabling, and Deleting Cookies

We remember your consent for 12 months. Then, the Cookie banner automatically reappears upon your first renewed visit to our Website. You can at any time change or withdraw your consent from the Cookie Declaration on this Website.

In addition, when you use Blocksi via a browser, you can change your web browser’s settings to reflect your ookie preferences. Each browser is a little different, but usually these settings are under the “options” or “preferences” menu. To learn more, please look at the Cookie settings available in your specific web browser(s). The links below will take you directly to your browser’s manual.
