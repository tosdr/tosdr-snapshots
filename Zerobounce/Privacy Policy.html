Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

When You visit the Site or otherwise interact with the Services, ZeroBounce may send one or more “cookies” to Your computer. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers stored on Your computer through Your web browser and are used by most websites to record user activity and to personalize Your web experience. Some cookies may facilitate Site features for enhanced performance and functionality such as remembering preferences, analyzing usage for Site optimization, and providing custom content. The length of time a cookie stays on Your device varies depending on the type of cookie.

What cookies does ZeroBounce use on the Site and why?

EU legislation requires websites to inform users when cookies are in use. Please note that strictly necessary cookies are being used by default, and you have consented to the use of such cookies upon your acceptance of the cookie consent banner language incorporated on our Site. We collect user consent to cookies every twelve months on our Site. We use the following types of cookies, including third party cookies, and similar technologies on this Site:

Strictly necessary cookies

Strictly necessary cookies are essential for us to deliver the Site and related Services to You securely and in compliance with applicable law and cannot be disabled. Such strictly necessary cookies include third party cookies, including but not limited to those from Amazon Web Services, Cloudflare, Okta, Stripe, and Zendesk. You cannot disable strictly necessary cookies. Without the strictly necessary cookies, you will be unable to login to Your ZeroBounce account and access the Services through the Site.

Targeting or advertising cookies

Targeting or advertising cookies help us deliver and track ads, and deliver relevant content for you. Such targeting or advertising cookies include third party cookies, including but not limited to those from Google/GoogleAdSense and Microsoft Bing. You may choose to disable these types of cookies.

ZeroBounce may link the information it stores in cookies to any Personal Information You submit through the Site or Services. ZeroBounce utilizes both session cookies, which remain active on Your web browser until closed, and persistent cookies, which are stored in Your web browser for a longer period of time, until they expire or are deleted.
