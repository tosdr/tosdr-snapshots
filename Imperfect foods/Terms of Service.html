25. Beet Dip Recipe

Congratulations! You're almost at the end of our Terms. As a reward, you've unlocked a Misfits Market must-try recipe in the form of a creamy, earthy beet dip.

First, you'll need:

  • 2 large beets (about 1 pound)
  • 1/2 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • Veggies or pita chips, for serving
  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees. Scrub the beets of any dirt and slice off the greens (but save for salad or pesto!). Wrap the beets in aluminum foil, place on a sheet pan, and roast for 45 minutes until tendera knife should easily pierce them. Let cool.
  2. Meanwhile, toast the walnuts in a skillet over medium heat until fragrant, tossing often so they don't burn, about 5 minutes.
  3. Rub off the beet skins with the help of a paper towel. Then, cut into 1-inch cubes.
  4. Put the beets, walnuts, feta, sour cream, garlic, and salt in the bowl of a food processor. Process until smooth.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Enjoy with fresh veggies like sliced cucumbers and carrot sticks, or pita chips.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
