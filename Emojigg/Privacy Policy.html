Intro

One of our main priorities at emoji.gg is maintaining and upholding the privacy of our users. Below we will disclose what information we store and how we use it.

Data we store

We store certain information provided by Discord, Slack or Twitch when you login to our website using their service.

  • Username/Display Name
  • Platform Unique ID
  • Email address
  • Avatar
How we use data

We use stored data to enable basic functionality of our website such as user profiles and asset attribution.
Data is never sold to any third parties.

Publicly accessible data

Some data we collect is accessible by the public. This is necessary for certain features of our website such as user profiles. Your username, profile picture and platform ID is considered publicly accessible data. We never share your email address.

Anonymously collected data

We collect some anonymous usage and browsing related data to enable functionality and better tailor content to our users. This includes downloads, search and browsing behaviour.

User Tracking

emoji.gg may track user and browsing behaviour on our website, this is done using Google Analytics. You can view their privacy policy here

Advertising

We use third party advertisements to support our site. Some of these advertisers may be served from our third party advertiser. You can view their privacy policy and cookie policy here. You can manage your cookies here.

We use Google Analytics and Comscore to analyse the use of our website. Google Analytics and Comscore gather information about website use by means of cookies. The information gathered relating to our website is used to create reports about the use of our website. Google's privacy policy is available at: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/. Comscore’s privacy policy is available at https://www.comscore.com/About-comScore/Privacy-Policy.

External Providers

emoji.gg uses several external providers to enable and enhance functionality on our website. Those are listed below along with their privacy policies.

  • Discord: Authentication | Privacy Policy
  • Slack: Authentication | Privacy Policy
  • Twitch: Authentication | Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: File caching | Privacy Policy
