Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of your personal data when you use our Service. 1. What data do we collect and how do we use them?

We process a few different types of information for different purposes as explained below.

Personal Data

(a) In order to provide our Service to you we process:
  • Your name and email address;
  • HTTPS requests to our Services, data destinations, and timestamps; however, we do not keep a record of the websites you visit, your IP address, the volume or nature of your network traffic, the content of any communications you make, or the duration of your online sessions.
(b) To manage payments for the Services we process your payment and transaction information (such as the name of the bank which issued the credit card uses for payments, partial credit card information, credit card’s owner’s full name, address, email,  chargebacks requests, cancelled orders and other information requested by legitimate our payment processors).

(c) For security monitoring, performance optimisation, debugging and troubleshooting, fraud detection and other operational intelligence purposes, we may collect and process our server logs containing your IP address, browser type, timestamp, and request URI; however, we cannot associate such data with you personally. This means that the such data is used in a way that doesn't allow us to identify who you are specifically. The information is typically aggregated or anonymized for analysis, so it serves a functional purpose without compromising your personal privacy.

(d) For the purposes of communicating with you and providing support, we process your email and communication information (e.g. content of your emails).

If you consent, we may use your Personal Data to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information that may be of interest to you. In such case we process your email. You may withdraw your consent to receive any, or all, of these communications from us by following the unsubscribe link or instructions provided in any email we send or by contacting us.

Personal Data

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and we hold certain information.

Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. Other tracking technologies such as beacons, tags and scripts are used to collect and track information to analyse and improve our Service.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some aspects of our Service.

Examples of Cookies we use:
  • Session Cookies. We use Session Cookies to operate our Service.
  • Preference Cookies. We use Preference Cookies to remember your preferences and various settings.
  • Security Cookies. We use Security Cookies for security purposes.
  • Advertising Cookies. Advertising Cookies are used to serve you with advertisements that may be relevant to you and your interests.
