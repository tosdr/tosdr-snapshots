What are cookies?

Cookies and similar tracking technologies, such as tags, scripts and pixels, are small pieces of code (“cookies”) that are stored on a device (computer, mobile phone, tablet etc) and which improve website functionality by remembering information about a user’s visit to the website.  These are stored on your device with your consent when you accept our cookie banner, amend your preferences, or continue to use our Website.

Cookies are used to collect information, where available, about your device, your IP address, operating system and browser type and how you have interacted with our websites.  We use them to remember your preferences and settings, analyze trends, administer the Website, track your interaction with the Website and to gather information about our Website users more generally

You can find more information about cookies at https://www.youronlinechoices.com/.

How to reject cookies

When you first visit our Website, you will be presented with a banner which offers you a choice about whether to accept or reject cookies of different types, which the exception of those cookies which are strictly necessary for use of the Website. You may also block cookies by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse the setting of all or some cookies. You can find information on how to manage cookie settings on certain browsers by following the following links:

  • Cookie settings in Chrome
  • Cookie settings in Firefox
  • Cookie settings in Internet Explorer
  • Cookie settings in Safari

To find out more about using your browser settings to control cookies, please visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org/

As noted above, cookie management tools provided by your browser will not remove Flash cookies. To learn how to manage privacy and storage settings for Flash cookies click here. To learn about how to disable Flash cookies click here.

Please note that if you block or limit cookies, you may not be able to use all of the features of the Website.
