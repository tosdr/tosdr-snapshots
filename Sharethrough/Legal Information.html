1. Introduction

Sharethrough, Inc., its affiliates, and subsidiaries (Sharethrough orwe or us our) respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

This User Privacy Notice (Privacy Notice) outlines our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing (Processing or Process) your Personal Data. Sharethrough may Process your Personal Data when you visit third-party websites, mobile applications and over-the-top (OTT) devices (such as SmartTVs or applications on your Smart TV) (Partner Sites), that use our advertising platform (the Advertising Platform) to serve targeted advertisements to you based on your personal interests (Advertisements).

As you navigate through and interact with Advertisements served by our Advertising Platform on Partner Sites, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment and also details of your interaction with Advertisements. This information includes personal information or personal data as defined under applicable law, including the laws of Canada, Quebec, Europe, the United Kingdom, the United States, including California and Virginia and more. Personal Data as referred to in this Privacy Notice is any information, whether combined or on its own, that directly or indirectly identifies or could reasonably identify you.

Please read this Privacy Notice carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your Personal Data and how we treat it. We recommend that you read this Privacy Notice in full; however, the links at the top of the page are intended to help you navigate to sections that are relevant to the information in which you may be interested.

This Privacy Notice applies to information we collect when you interact with Advertisements on Partner Sites. It does not apply to:

  • Information collected by Buyers, other providers, or any third parties with whom we conduct business;
  • Information related to your employment or potential employment with us. This can be found on our Candidate Privacy Notice;
  • Information collected by our website. This can be found on our Website Privacy Notice; or
  • Information we collect in our business relationship with you. This can be found on our Corporate Privacy Notice.

We recommend that you also consult the privacy policies of the third-party digital properties you use to become familiar with their privacy and data collection uses and practices.

2.About Us

Sharethrough is a supply-side platform (SSP) and advertising exchange. Our technology enables us to conduct online auctions for advertising space on Partner Sites. These third-party partners are collectively referred to as Partners in this Privacy Notice.

Through the Advertising Platform, Sharethrough connects Partner Sites to buyers of advertising space that wish to bid on Partner ad space inventory. Buyers include advertisers and intermediary demand-side partners (collectively, Buyers). Buyers work with Sharethrough based on contractual relationships to deliver ads based on your personal interests and to reach their target audiences.

3. The Information We Collect

Information Sharethrough and our Partners collect

Sharethrough and our partners (Partners, Buyers, and any other type of business involved in the ad tech ecosystem) operate on an open-source protocol to provide our services, known as Open RTB. This open-source protocol allows the collection of information such as:

  • Cookie identifiers – A cookie is a small file placed on the browser of your computer.
    • Sharethrough cookies
      • Our Advertising Platform uses persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide our services. These cookies are used to display promotions or advertisements on Partner Sites based on your interests. They collect information about your activities on our Partner Sites and other sites to provide you with Advertisements that match your interests.
      • We may collect and store cookie identifiers from a variety of domains, and store such identifiers at either our domain or our partners domains. We also engage in cookie syncing, which is the matching of our cookie identifiers with those of our clients and other partners.
  • Third party cookies
    • In addition to Sharethrough cookies, Buyers may also drop persistent cookies on your browser. Buyers include companies such as Google to deliver the best advertising opportunities to its clients. Traffic Clients and other data subjects can consult Googles policies to learn more about Googles processing policies. In particular, Google may use remarketing tags (please visit https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites to learn more about the third parties Google works with) and other advertising cookies.
  • Web Beacons– These are small electronic files on Partner Sites (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit our Advertising Platform, to perform activities, such as counting users who interacted with certain Advertisements and understanding the scope of that interaction.

  • Clickstream data This includes the Advertisements you have clicked or that have otherwise been viewable to you, the amount of Advertisements displayed and the amount of time the Advertisement was viewable to you.

  • Device information As applicable, general information such as device type (e.g., laptop, mobile, TV), model, manufacturer, operating system, time zone, telephone carrier information, network information (e.g., WIFI), and mobile application information. Mobile device information includes standard mobile advertising identifiers located on every mobile device. These are:
    • IDFA advertising identifier used on Apples iOS devices; and
    • Google Advertising ID(AAIDorGAID) advertising identifier used on Android devices, to track your use of mobile apps on your device(s).
  • Geolocation data – Location information such as city, country, zip code/postal code, and/or longitude/latitude.
  • Date and time of your visit to a Partner Site
  • IP address
  • User agent – A short bit of text that attempts to describe the software/browser (the “Agent“) that is making the request to a website. The user agent often includes descriptions of the operating system and device type that the Agent is running on.
  • Information about your internet connection and, and other communication data and the resources that may be a result of your access to Advertisements on Partner Sites.

The above is a comprehensive but non-exhaustive list of the personal data we may collect and or receive about you. The full list can be found on the Open RTB protocol spec, as amended from time to time.

Sharethrough may Process your Personal Data to:

  • Engage in real time transactions for the sale of Advertisements and deliver such ads to you on our Partner Sites;
  • Show you Advertisements on the Partner Sites that are more relevant to your interests(for example, Advertisements based on other websites and online platforms that you have visited);

  • Detect and stop click fraud on the Partner Sites;
  • Audit your interactions with Advertisements, including, but not limited to: counting advertising impressions to unique visitors, verifying positioning and quality of ad impressions, and auditing compliance with any applicable specification and other standards;
  • Help maintain the safety, security, and integrity of Sharethroughs Advertising Platform, detect security incidents, protect against malicious, deceptive, fraudulent, or illegal activity related to the use of the Advertising Platform;
  • Identify and repair errors that impair existing intended functionality in the Advertising Platform;
  • Carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between the operators of our Partner Sites and Buyers;
  • Conduct internal testing, research, analysis, reporting, and product development, including to develop and improve our Advertising Platform; and
  • Process your Personal Data for any other purpose with your consent.

Kindly note, Sharethrough does not measure your browser history, or engage in retargeting, reidentification, or audience segmentation; however, some of our partners may engage in these practices. They may also Process your Personal Data. In addition, Sharethrough does not participate in cross-device linking. Cross device linking is the process of tracking a user across multiple devices, for example, across a SmartTV, mobile, and/or website; some of our partners may engage in cross device linking.
