Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16th, 2023

At W. Atlee Burpee Company, we respect your privacy and remain committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. We understand that you care about how information about you is collected, used, and shared. Keeping you informed about the data we collect and the choices you may have regarding your information is a top priority.

This privacy policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website (www.burpee.com) (the “Website”) or when you download and use the Burpee Garden Time Planner Mobile Application (the “App,” and together with the Website, the “Platform”) and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information. Our Platform provides our visitors access to a wide range of information, inspiration, and products including novel and traditional vegetables, herbs, fruits, flowers, perennials, and quality gardening supplies (collectively, the “Services”). 

By accessing our Platform or providing information to us in any other way, you understand that such information will be processed in the manner described in this privacy policy. Additionally, your access to and use of the Platform and the Services is subject to our Term & Conditions (https://www.burpee.com/terms-and-conditions) which are incorporated herein. 

 

This policy applies to information we collect:

  • On our Platform; and
  • In email, text, and other electronic messages between you and us.

It does not apply to information collected by:

  • Us offline or through any other means; or
  • Any third party including through any application or content that may be accessible on our Platform.

It is our goal to be transparent about the use of your information so please read this policy carefully to understand our policies and practices. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Platform. This policy may change from time to time. Your continued use of our Platform after we make changes is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the policy periodically for updates.


Information We Collect About You and How We Collect It

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Platform, including information by which you may be personally identified, such as your name, postal address, e-mail address, and other online identifiers such as your IP address. Our Platform also automatically collects certain information about its users, including information about your use of our Platform, your internet connection, the device you use to visit our Platform, the features that you access, and other usage details. 

 

We collect this information:

The information we collect on or through our Platform may include:

  • Details of transactions you carry out through our Platform and the fulfillment of your orders. 

 

The information we collect automatically may include personal information, and we may maintain it or associate it with personal information we collect in other ways. It helps us to improve our Platform and Services and to deliver a better and more personalized service, including by enabling us to:

Data Privacy Rights

You may have certain rights with respect to your personal information. If you which to exercise a data privacy right provided to you by your state of residence, please reach out to us in the manners described below. Please note that all such requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis and depending upon the applicable law, your request may be denied.
Your data privacy rights may include the following:

  • The Right to Know and Data Portability Rights. You may have the right to know what personal information we have collected and request specific pieces of your personal information, the purposes for which that information has been collected, the sources of that information, and the categories of third parties with whom we have shared that information.
  • The Right to Delete. You may request that we delete your personal information that we have collected from you and direct us to tell our services providers to do the same. Please note, that there are many exceptions that may prevent us from complying with your request to delete.
  • The Right to Opt-Out. You may have the right to direct us to not sell your information or to share it for cross-context advertising.
  • The Right to Correct. You may have the right to ask us to correct inaccurate information that we have about you.
  • The Right to Limit. You may have the right to direct us to limit the processing of your information that has been determined to be “sensitive” by applicable law.
  • The Right to Non-Discrimination. We will not deny or provide you with a different level of quality of goods or service because you have exercised your data privacy rights.
  • The Right of Appeal. You may have the right to appeal our decision to deny your data privacy request.

In order to exercise your data privacy rights, you must provide sufficient information to enable us to reasonably verify that you are the person about whom we have collected personal information. Additionally, you must describe your request with sufficient detail to allow us to properly understand, evaluate, and respond to it.

As permitted by applicable law, you may have an authorized agent submit a request on your behalf. However, we may require proof of such agent’s status as an authorized agent, including an executed permission by you and verification of the agent’s own identity.
