    Our promise to you

    At First Group Enterprises Ltd, we are committed to protecting the privacy of all customers and visitors to our websites.  Our privacy policy covers areas that, as users and parents ourselves, we would like explained clearly.

    First Group Enterprises Ltd is the owner of First News, First Wonder Box and First Careers.  We are a family owned business and the sole owner of information collected on this site. We are also the data controller for information required to process orders.

    The only personal data we collect from you is the information required to process your order and manage your subscription in the most effective way. This includes name, address and date of birth details of the children receiving our products.   This information is required to ensure that we are providing relevant products that are age-appropriate.

    We do not share your personal data with any third parties other than those involved in the administration of your subscription e.g. our mailing house or a customer service agent.  All our contracted processors of personal information have signed a contract with us to provide at least the same processes to protect your personal data.

    We will never knowingly contact any child directly, other than to deliver the product that has been ordered.

    We will always process personal data securely and in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation, 25 May 2018.

    At the end of your subscription term, we will hold your data for a period of 3 years and transaction details for 6 years plus 1 in accordance with the HMRC regulations.  This enables us to renew your subscription easily, should you choose to re-subscribe. It also enables us to remind you that your subscription has lapsed and to offer you preferential rates or alternative products.   After this time we will delete the data we hold on you.

    We will not market to you beyond 3 years after your subscription has lapsed and any communications we do send are to ensure you don’t miss out on promotions about new products that might interest you or the children in your lives.

     

     

    Our Privacy Policy in detail

     Subscription data

     

    The information we collect

    When taking orders, we process your data within the lawful basis of ‘contractual obligation’.  This means we only take information from you which is required in order to process your subscription and give you (or the recipient) the best possible customer experience.  We will ask you to submit the name and delivery address of the recipient (often a child under 16 years of age) and the name, address and contact details (phone number and email address) of the person placing the order (the purchaser).  For First Wonder Box orders we also ask for the month and year of birth, plus the gender of the child.  We take this information so that we can tailor boxes to the demographic of our customers later on in the subscription series and recommend other First Group Enterprise Ltd products, which may be of interest.  We believe this will give our customers the best experience of our product.

     

    Our lawful basis for processing your personal data.

    We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

    • Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests (or those of a third party) and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.
    • Where we need to perform the contract we are about to enter into or have entered into with you.
    • Where we need to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.
    • Where we have your consent

    Legitimate Interest means the interest of our business to enable us to give the best service possible to the recipients and purchasers of our products. We make sure we consider and balance any potential impact on you (both positive and negative) and your rights before we process your personal data for our legitimate interests. We do not use your personal data for activities where our interests are overridden by the impact on you (unless we have your consent or are otherwise required or permitted to by law). You can obtain further information about how we assess our legitimate interests against any potential impact on you in respect of specific activities by contacting us at [email protected]

    As required under GDPR we have conducted a Legitimate Interest Assessment in relation to sending marketing material.  We assessed the balance of your rights with ours and we believe that you will not be disadvantaged by receiving our communications and we can demonstrate under GDPR that we have a legitimate interest in using your data for marketing purposes.   You always have a choice of opting out or unsubscribing or by contacting us at [email protected]

     

    How we use your data

    The data we collect is used to process orders.  We will never contact a child directly (other than the physical delivery of their product).  All other correspondence will be conducted directly with the purchaser.  We will store the contact details that were given to us at the time of purchase (unless these are later changed by you). This is to ensure a good service and effective communication.  The purchaser may be contacted regarding delivery issues, changes to the service, changes in payment terms or any other service related issues.

    You will be given a straight-forward way to opt-out of our marketing communications each time you are contacted.

    We may share your personal data with a third-party auditing organisation (ABC) so they can verify aggregated statistics about circulation and usage of our products or review our policies, processes and procedures for compliance with relevant standards. You can view their privacy policy at www.abc.org.uk/privacy-policy

     

    Storage of data

    Data is stored securely with First Group Enterprises Ltd and our thoroughly vetted third parties. These third parties are essential to the processing of orders.  Whilst First Group Enterprises Ltd is the controller of the data, our third party suppliers also strictly follow the GDPR legislation dated 25 May 2018.  Data is not shared beyond our contracted third parties.

    At the end of your subscription term, we will store your data for a period of 3 years. This enables us to renew your subscription easily, should you choose to re-subscribe. It also enables us to remind you that your subscription has lapsed, offer you preferential rates and to recommend other relevant First Group Enterprises Ltd products that we think may be of interest to you.  We also store your transaction details for 6 years plus 1 in accordance with the HMRC regulations.

    Your rights

    You have some improved rights under the GDPR.

    • Data Subject Access Request:  You have the right to access the personal information we may hold about you. On receipt of such a request we will endeavour to respond to you as soon as possible, but at least within one calendar month.  You must provide us with 2 forms of personal identity to ensure that we only disclose to you information which is relevant to you personally. You will not have to pay a fee to access your personal data (or to exercise any of the other rights). However, we may charge a reasonable fee if your request is clearly unfounded, repetitive or excessive. Alternatively, we may refuse to comply with your request in these circumstances.

    • Erasure: You have the right to request that we delete any personal information pertaining to you. Any questions about these rights may be sent to [email protected].

    Competition Data

     

    What data do we collect?

    In order to enter competitions online, entrants must register via our website www.firstnews.co.uk.  Registration data required includes name, age bracket, address, email address and phone number. We also ask for parent/carer’s details (name, address, email and phone number).  A notification is sent by email to the parent (or carer) when a child requests to register on the website.  The parent/carer is required to approve this in order for the registration to be complete and for the child to participate in any activity.

    Why do we collect it?

    Competition data is collected so that winners can be notified if they have won a competition. This is normally sent via email to the parent, but in some cases we contact the child directly, by means of a letter and/or congratulations certificate, along with their prize. Age bracket – for example 13-15 – is required, as some competition prizes have age restrictions.

    We do not share your personal data with any third parties other than those involved in the administration and prize fulfilment.

    How do we store it?

    If a competition has been entered by post, the physical entry is kept in a locked cupboard and then shredded once the competition winner has been selected and notified. If the entry is made via our website, the details are stored securely in the back end of the website and then deleted from our systems once the winner has been notified.

    Winners’ details are kept in a locked folder on our server, only the competition manager has access to this.

    Why do we store winners’ data and for how long?

    We keep winners’ details to allow First Group Enterprises Ltd and prize providers to contact winners and ensure prizes are delivered and received as per each individual competition prize offered.

    Winners’ entries are kept for six months and then all data is deleted/shredded. All other competition entries are deleted from the system or website and/or shredded once the winner has been notified and the prize accepted.
