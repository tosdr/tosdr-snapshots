hear.com (we, us, our) presents this privacy policy to describe how we collect, use, and share information through our website and our other digital properties that link to it.

Information Collection

We collect information in two ways: information you provide to us and information we automatically collect when you access our digital property. We may combine this information together.

Information You Provide to Us. When you engage with our digital properties you may provide us certain information about yourself. This information may include your name, email, phone number, and postal address. We also collect information about your payment method, transaction history, health insurance, and other information about your medical history or visits with certain medical professionals when you provide it to us or they provide it to us on your behalf.

Information We Automatically Collect. This data reflects your usage of the website and our services. This information includes digital identifiers like cookie and device identifiers, IP addresses, browser type, date and time of your visit, and your interactions and page views while interacting with our digital property. We also make inferences about your potential interests based on this viewing activity. We typically collect this type of information using cookies, web beacons, clear gifs, and other similar technologies.

Information Use and Sharing

We use the information we collect about you for a variety of purposes, including:

  • To present our websites and digital properties to you;
  • To authenticate users and remember preferences;
  • To provide you with information, products, or services that you request from us;
  • To provide you with information about our products and services we believe are relevant to your interest;
  • To provide you with additional information about our services;
  • To carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered between you and us, including for appointment confirmation, scheduling, billing, shipping, financing, or transaction information;
  • Internal auditing and debugging;
  • Product and service improvement and development;
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features on our website;
  • In any other way we may describe when you provide the information; and
  • For any other purpose with your consent.

We may share or disclose information we collect about you with several entities, including:

  • With local audiologists, hearing aid manufacturers, financing companies, and health insurance companies to fulfil your requests and orders;
  • To our affiliates and subsidiaries;
  • To vendors and service providers that help us provide the website and services;
  • To third parties to help advertiser our services;
  • To a buyer or other successor in the event of a merger, divestiture, restructuring, reorganization, dissolution, or other sale or transfer of some or all the Companys assets, including at bankruptcy;
  • To fulfill the purpose for which you provide it, such as communicating with you via email or other means; and
  • For any other purpose disclosed by us when you provide the information.
  • In response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.

Data obtained through the short code program will not be shared with any third-parties for their marketing reasons/purposes.

Third Party Websites

This privacy policy addresses the collection, use, and disclosure of information we collect from you on our website or digital property that links to the policy. Other websites that may be accessible through this website have their own privacy policies and data collection, use and disclosure practices. If you link to any such website, we urge you review the websites privacy policy.

Ad Choices

We may partner with ad networks and other ad serving providers (Advertising Providers) who serve ads on behalf of us and others on non-affiliated sites. Some of those ads may be personalized, meaning that they are intended to be relevant to you based on information Advertising Providers collect about your visits to this website and elsewhere on the Internet over time, as well as from various mobile applications you use, and across different associated devices. This is called interest-based advertising. Interest-based advertising includes when Advertising Partners identify possible relationships among different browsers and devices.

You may visit https://optout.aboutads.info/ to learn more about this type of advertising and how to opt-out of this type of advertising by companies participating in the Digital Advertising Alliance self-regulatory program. Additionally, if you would like to opt-out of this type of advertising in mobile apps, you can visit https://youradchoices.com/appchoices and download the appropriate app for your device.

We adhere to the Digital Advertising Alliances Self-Regulatory Principles. Note that electing to opt out will not stop advertising from appearing in your browser or applications. It may make the ads you see less relevant to your interests. In addition, note that if you use a different browser or devices, erase cookies, or reset a device identifier from your browser or mobile device, you may need to renew you opt-out choice.

Updating Information
