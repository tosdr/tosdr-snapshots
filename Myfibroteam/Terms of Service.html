Description of Services

MyFibroTeam ("MyFibroTeam," "MyFibroTeam Mobile," "us" or "Site") is a free resource for the fibromyalgia community to share and connect with others and exchange insights about providers. It is a destination to:

  • find other people like you in order to connect and share;
  • read about the teams of providers assembled by people like you;
  • read the personal stories of others to better understand the context in which those teams were assembled;
  • share information about their own stories and teams and reviews of specific providers they may be looking for or have experience with; and
  • communicate with others in the community to share tips and ask questions.
