Short version

We value your privacy and strive to protect it. In this privacy statement we’ll explain which personal data is collected and why it is being processed. The (personal) data we process will never be shared with third parties without your explicit prior consent. You’ll find the highlights in this short summary, but we recommend you read the full version carefully.

We collect all information, including personal data, that you provide us with. Aside from that, we also store technical information like cookies to make sure you experience on the website and the platform is as comfortable and personal as possible. All data is stored in secured servers that are not accessible to unauthorized personnel.

The platform uses several technological mechanisms to automate our processes. This includes automated decision-making based on profile and experience. An example of this is the eligibility of a mystery shopper for a certain assignment.

If you would like to exercise your right to object and/or your right to data portability, or do you have any other questions about our data processing? Please contact us with a specific request at support@secretview.io.

Long version

We process data from the following groups:

  1. Visitors of our website www.secretview.io;
  2. People registered as mystery shoppers with Secret View;
  3. Contacts of Secret View’s clients.

Below we will outline per group the personal data we process and why we do so.

  1. Website visitors

We process visitors’ personal data on our website www.secretview.io by placing cookies and similar technologies such as web beacons.

A cookie is a small text file that is stored in your computer’s browser, tablet or smartphone when you first visit the website. We use functional, analytical and tracking cookies. On your first visit, we’ve informed you of these cookies and asked for your permission to store them in your browser. Please find all information about our cookies in our Cookie Policy.

  1. Community members

When you register as a community member on our platform, you engage in an agreement with us. To carry out this agreement, e.g., do mystery shopping research, it is necessary for us to process some of your personal data. We’ll sum up the data we process:

-          Name

-          Sex

-          Date of birth

-          Address

-          Phone number

-          E-mail address

-          Other data that is actively provided by creating a profile, in writing or by phone

-          Location details

-          Banking details

-          Financial remunerations

Without these details we are unable to hold our part of the agreement. In order to fulfill our legal and societal duties we will process data in the event of (suspected) cases of illegal activities by members of our community and provide them to authorities. Also, we store verified addresses by using iDIN (https://www.idin.nl/disclaimer-privacy/) in order to be able to take legal action in the event of illegal activities.

Why do we process personal data?

We process your personal data to:

-          Allow you to create an online account

-          Assign one or more mystery shopping assignments to you

-          Communicate with you in relation to the assignments

-          Handle payments

-          Verify your age when you apply for an age-related assignment that legally requires us to verify your age (e.g. alcohol and tobacco sales compliance research)

-          Analyze user behavior on our website and platform to improve our online tools and to adapt our services to your preferences

-          Being able to create analyses and anonymized statistics on the basis of your age, income and other data you have shared with us, that helps us inform our clients (for example: “mystery shoppers between 40- and 45-years old rate your store the highest”);

-          Follow up on complaints and questions

-          Carry out legal duties or court rulings, such as providing data for tax returns

-          Preventing misuse and abuse

What else do we do with your data?

To make your experience as a mystery shopper as good as possible, your data is saved in your online account. This way you won’t need to enter your data repeatedly.

Community Approval

The results from our researches go through a quality control before they are validated and sent to our clients. This approval process is carried out by Secret View employees, but also by other members of the community (mystery shoppers). Community members with this capacity are called Community Approvers and are carefully selected by us. Therefore, it is possible that another community member gets to view the questionnaire you send in. We take every possible step to decrease the chance a Community Approver gets insight into personal data. Your questionnaire is anonymized and there is a blackout tool that can be used to blur out certain sections. However, you are personally responsible for the information you provide textually in your answers about yourself or other people in describing certain situations. The Community Approver can edit and/or improve the answers you provide.

Automated decision-making

Using automated decision-making processes allows us to more efficiently handle large numbers of (mystery shopping) assignments. These automated processes may have consequences for registered community members regarding planning and/or carrying out assignments. The processes lead to decision made by computer programs or systems without human (i.e., a Secret View employee) interference/involvement.

We use the following computer programs and systems:

Name:

Self-developed software

Why do we use it?

The software we develop in-house is used to make carrying out research and insights into its results as efficient and user-friendly as possible.

What automated process take place in the software?

The following processes may be (to some extent) automated:

  • Profile and account management
  • Insight into all assignments
  • Selection of mystery shoppers
  • Insight into assignment status
  • Information supply for assignments
  • Handling payments

To make the transition period from manual to automated processes as smooth as possible, every automation (process, decision-making, specific actions) is first rolled out in phases. The automated process, decision or action will first be shown to the employee and/or user, who will manually have to confirm the event. Only when we are sure the automation works as intended at all times, we will allow the automation to take place without human interference. Using this method, we make sure users will not notice the transition from manual to automation – except for the efficiency and user-friendliness increases.

As a community member, you have the right to voice your opinion with regards to an automated decision that involves you, and to object to this decision. If you do not agree with an automated decision, please feel free to contact us. An employee will manually review the decision. By agreeing to carry out assignments (and our Terms & Conditions and this Privacy Policy), you explicitly agree with the use of automated decision-making processes in our platform.

How long do we store personal data?

We do not store your data any longer than is strictly necessary to achieve the goals they are collected for. Moreover, you can delete your account if you wish to do so, unless there is still an assignment to be carried out in that you are scheduled for.

If you exercise this right to be forgotten, your profile we be deleted from our platform immediately. All other personal data will be deleted after sending a request by e-mail to mysteryshopping@secretview.io. All personal data will be deleted within the space of two months unless the data is required to comply with government regulations. With regards to the latter, we maintain a 7-year storage period due to fiscal obligations.

Your rights

You have the right to get insight into your personal data, but also to edit and to delete it. You are free to do so through the personal settings in your account on our platform. Moreover, you have the right to retract your permission to process your personal data or to object to our processing of your data. Also, you have the right to data portability. That means you can ask us to transfer your data in a common format to another organization of your choosing.

Should you like to exercise one or more of these rights, we may ask you to verify your identity via two-factor authentication using e-mail and SMS. You will be sent two codes will have to be verified with Secret View. After this verification, we will grant your request.

We will try to respond as quickly as possible, but within four weeks at the most. We would also like to point out the option of issuing a complaint with the Dutch national authority, the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) through the following link: https://autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl/nl/contact-met-de-autoriteit-persoonsgegevens/tip-ons.

How we protect personal data

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously and take fitting measures to prevent and counter abuse, loss, unauthorized access, unwanted publication, and illicit editing of the data.

Please find the most important measures below:

-          Keeping the online infrastructure updated

-          Use of security groups

-          Data encryption

-          Segmented insight into data

-          Use of HTTPS in both external and internal communication

-          Database back-ups

-          GIT combined with CI/CD for efficient patch management continuation

-          Stimulating privacy protection awareness with employees

-          Testing, rating, and evaluating procedures for protective measures

-          Protocol formulation in case of data leaks or security incidents

-          Confidentiality agreements

-          Processor agreements

-          Minimizing contact with personal data

-          Keeping track of all data processing activities

-          Maintaining a data vault

-          Minimizing reception of personal data by clients

Should you feel your data is not properly protected or there are signs of abuse/misuse of your data, please contact our team at mysteryshopping@secretview.io.

Complaints

You have the right to file a complaint with the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens (AP) if you feel your data is not being used properly.

Modifications
