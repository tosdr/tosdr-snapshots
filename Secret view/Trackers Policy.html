Yum, cookies!

25-01-2022

We may use cookies to enhance your user experience on our website and platform. Cookies are small bits of information that are saved on your computer while you browse the website and platform. Cookies make sure you can be recognized when you log in, they help us make the pages load faster, save your data in a safe place and allow us to detect bugs and other errors in our website and software.

Don't want to receive cookies? You can delete them in your browser settings. Remember that the software may be slower and not perform as well!

You consent to our cookies if you continue to use our website. The law states that we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies, we need your permission. This site uses different types of cookies. Some cookies are placed by third party services that appear on our pages.

You can at any time change or withdraw your consent from the Cookie Declaration on our website.

Functional cookies

We use cookies with a purely functional purpose. These ensure our website performs as intended and your preferences are saved.

Analytical cookies

We try to analyze the behavior of visitors on our website. This way we try to determine our visitors’ interest, maintain, and optimize the website’s functionality. That is why we store analytical cookies in your browser.

Using these cookies we collect information about your computer, operating system, browser, statistics on pageviews, traffic to and from the website, location, the referring URL, and the IP address.

Google Analytics and Application Insights

In order to analyze the data, we use the services of Google Analytics and Application Insights. To that end we have processor agreements with these parties. We guarantee that whenever personal data is processed that data is encrypted. Also, the last eight numbers of the IP address are hidden.

Tracking cookies and third-party cookies

We also store cookies that track your behavior on the internet so we can provide you with content and adverts that are relevant to you.

Google Maps

Our website and the platform may use Google Maps. This allows us to display geographical maps and travel directions on websites. To use this service, we need to place several cookies to remember your preferences.

Social media

Our website may contain buttons to promote or share (“like”, “tweet”, “share”) content and/or pages to social media such as LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. These buttons contain small pieces of code that store cookies we cannot influence. We recommend reading the respective privacy statements of these companies to find out how they process personal data.

LinkedIn:                                https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy

Twitter:                                  https://twitter.com/privacy/         

Facebook (also Instagram): http://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/        

The information gathered by these parties is anonymized as much as possible. The information is transferred to LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter and may be stored on servers outside of the European Union.

Cookie preferences

You may edit your cookie preferences so that they are no longer stored on your computer. Please go to the bottom of this text to edit your preferences. Moreover, you can delete all information that has previously been stored by navigating to your browser’s settings.

Without cookies, some parts of the website and platform may not work properly or work at all.
