Privacy Policy

Welcome to Discadia, your privacy is crucial to us and our operation. If you have any questions concerning specifics regarding this privacy policy document please join our official discord support server and make a ticket. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

To start off, we will only use your information to improve our service for the user, it should be noted we will not share or sell your data to any third parties, but we will simply use any data gathered to improve our website in house.

By using our service, you are by default agreeing to this policy and hence agreeing to discadia.com processing your data with the intentions provided in this privacy policy document.

Information we collect

When you use our site, there are several automatic information collection processes in place in accordance with the proper functioning of the site. These include but are not limited to username collection, ip address collection, useragent & operating system collection, e-mail addresses, user messages & comments. This aforementioned data will be stored in secure databases maintained by us.

You may also choose to provide us with extra information; such as deciding to link facebook & twitter accounts. By doing this you automatically grant us permission to extract relevant information from these connected assets.

We employ the usage of cookies on our site for general functioning and ease of functionality, such as us being able to keep track of what account you are logged into; cookies are very small bits of data stored on your browser that allow us to keep track of your preferences and account when you use our site. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser if you wish, however it is not guaranteed our site will function as designed if you choose to do so.

Any data collected is also subject to analysis of demographics and user behaviour; by using our site, you agree to partake in this. We may disclose anonymized statistics to third parties and business partners at our discretion.

We use third party services such as Google analytics in contribution to optimal functioning and maintenance of our site, services such as these may use cookies in order to function. If you do not agree to this or would rather not take part in this; you may choose to opt-out of usage of these third party services via their respective methods of doing so. discadia.com is based in the United States of America, thereby using our site you agree to all relevant digital millennium applicable laws and more broadly all data and privacy laws set out by said region. All data will be processed and stored in the United States of America.

Use of information

All information is processed in accordance with this privacy policy, again if you do not feel comfortable with this, then please do not use our site. All information we collect goes towards the optimal function of the site, aggregation of data so we and our business partners can make informed decisions and for preventing abuse of our systems (e.g. - preventing vote cheating & list spamming). Please keep in mind however, all your personal information is NEVER sold to any other third party or distributed to anyone else. We keep this information highly secure at our utmost priority.

Data Retention

By using our site, you agree to the fact we hold the right to store any aforementioned data for as long as necessary pertaining to the proper functioning of our website. If you would like to dispose of or request your personal data copy as per EU GDPR regulations; you may join our discord support server and ask us to do so. We will reply as soon as possible.

At discadia.com we take all relevant security measures in order to ensure your data is fully protected. We do not store data with third parties and we maintain secure databases. However it should be kept in mind this is not a guarantee and we cannot guarantee by any means 100% security of all information collected, nor can any website or service on the internet. Therefore we cannot guarantee any level of security.

Regarding Minors

Please note it is against our ToS to use our website under the age of 13, as it is also against the Discord ToS to use said service under the age of 13. We do not willingly or knowingly maintain or collect the data of users under the age of 13 If you are under the age of 13 please do not use our website and please exit immediately. In any event that we learn we have collected the data of a child under 13 we will delete this data immediately and should we find out a user of our site is under 13 we reserve the right to IP blacklist this user and suspend their account and any listings. If you believe someone under the age of 13 is using our site, please join our discord support server to let us know and we will deal with it appropriately.

Other queries/ Contact Info

If you have any other questions regarding this privacy document or any concerns in general, please do not hesitate to join our support discord server and we will do our best to get back to you as soon as possible.

Cheers and have fun!
