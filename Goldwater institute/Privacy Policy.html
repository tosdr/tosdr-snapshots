Privacy Policy

The Goldwater Institute’s Web server does not automatically recognize a visitor’s e-mail address. We do collect information on which pages visitors access or view, and store information volunteered by the consumer, such as site registrations. The information we collect is used to improve the content of our Web page. We use cookies to record past activity on our site in order to provide better service when visitors return to our site.

If you sign up to receive email but later decide you do not want to receive e-mail , please send an email requesting to be removed from the email list.

If you supply us with your postal address you may receive periodic mailings with information on new policy reports or upcoming events. If you do not wish to receive postal mail, please send an email requesting to be removed from the mailing list.
