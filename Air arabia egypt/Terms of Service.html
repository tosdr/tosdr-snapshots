  2. Membership/Program Registration
    1. AirRewards is a card less, customer loyalty Program. Program registration is available online at www.airarabia.com/register-now or through the Air Arabia call centre at +971 6 558 0000 in Sharjah, UAE, +212 80 2000803 in Casablanca, Morocco.
    2. Membership to AirRewards is for individuals only. All other entities or parties including, but not limited to, corporations, travel agencies, etc., are not eligible to enrol as Members or earn Points or receive any benefits in connection with the Program. Members must enrol as individuals and may not pool or combine Points with other Members, unless such Members are part of the same Family Account. Each Member may have only one (1) AirRewards account. If a member is less than 13 years of age, enrolment must be completed by a parent or guardian on behalf of the member.
    3. Each Member must provide the basic required information and valid individual e-mail address in order to register and enrol in AirRewards. Requests for name changes may require supporting legal documentation.
    4. While Air Arabia issues monthly electronic statements for AirRewards accounts, each Member shall be responsible for staying knowledgeable about their account information including, but not limited to, accumulated Points, redeemed Points and the Program Rules. Account information can be found at www.airarabia.com/air-rewards-loyalty-program.
    5. AirRewards Members from the same family may elect to pool their AirRewards Points by starting a Family Account. One person from the family must be designated as the Head of Household. Each Family Member must update the Head of Household AirRewards ID in their account. The Head of Household may have a maximum of eight Family Members enrolled into the Family Account.
    6. The Head of Household is required to be a minimum of 21 years of age.
    7. Persons eligible to become Family Members are (with respect to the Head of Household): brother(s), sister(s), father, mother, grand father, grand mother, spouse(s), and children.
    8. We reserve the right to audit the relationships claimed by any Head of Household and we may from time to time request documented proof of the relationships. In the case where the relevant documentation is not provided, the miles accrued by the family member to the Family account will be forfeited.
    9. A Family Member may only be registered in respect of one Family Account. A Head of Household can only be registered in respect of one Family Account.
    10. The Head of Household and each Family Member must be existing AirRewards Members (otherwise they must first become Members following which they can start or be join a Family Account (as the case may be). Once listed as a Family Member, such members cannot be replaced for a period of 12 Months.
    11. 50% of all AirRewards Points earned by a Family Member will be transferred to and accumulated under the AirRewards account of the Head of Household. The rest 50% will be forfeited.
    12. The Head of Household maintains exclusive control of the Family account and as such controls all redemptions made through this account. We are not obliged to honour or act upon any request by a Family Member for redemption of AirRewards points.
    13. We retain sole discretion to accept or reject any application for Family Account and to cancel any Member’s AirRewards membership account and any accrued points in the event of breach of these Terms and Conditions.
       
  3. Accumulation/Earning Points
    1. Members may earn Points in the following ways: (1) Purchasing an Air Arabia flight (2) Purchasing ancillary services offered by Air Arabia namely baggage allowance, meal, insurance, seat selection and Hala services. (3) Purchasing products/services from our retail partners.
    2. Members will earn five (5) Points per USD (or equivalent) spent on flights and baggage allowance; and ten (10) Points per USD (or equivalent) spent on meal selection, insurance, seat selection and Hala services. Points can be earned on the base fare and surcharge of the flight only and on the value of ancillary services purchased. Points cannot be earned on government taxes/fees, or other fees or charges. Members will only earn Points based on the eligible amount spent on individual flights. Members will not earn any Points for amounts spent on any other traveller’s flight, even if this traveller is on the Member’s booking and/or whose flight has been paid for by the Member. If a Member is traveling with other AirRewards Members, each Member's AirRewards account information must be entered at the time of booking in order for every Member to earn Points on their individual Air Arabia flight. For a flight paid for partly using points, points cannot be earned on the portion paid using AirRewards points.
    3. Air Arabia may, in its sole discretion, from time to time, offer Members the opportunity to earn additional Points through the purchase of Air Arabia flights and ancillary products or services. These offers may be temporary in nature. Air Arabia reserves the right to modify or eliminate such offers at any time. These offers are void where prohibited by law. The amount of Points offered is subject to Air Arabia's sole discretion. All special offers involving Points or the Program are subject to these Program Rules.
    4. There may be some delay between eligible transactions and the posting of Points in a Member’s AirRewards account.
    5. To receive Points for eligible activities, the name and AirRewards registration details provided in connection with the reservation or purchase must exactly match the name and AirRewards account details registered with Air Arabia. For example, and without in any way limiting the generality of the foregoing, for a flight purchase, the name associated with the AirRewards account provided for that flight purchase must exactly match the name of the traveller. Any discrepancy may delay or nullify the receipt of Points.
    6. To receive Points for the purchase of Air Arabia flights, Members must provide their AirRewards id either at the time of purchase through www.airarabia.com; at the time of purchase or before the flight through the Air Arabia Contact centre, authorized sales shop or travel agency; or retroactively, at www.airarabia.com/air-rewards-loyalty-program pursuant to the following: Any claim for missing Points must be received by AirRewards within 3 months (90 days) of the qualifying Air Arabia flight. Members are responsible for ensuring that Points earned are credited to their AirRewards account. If Members believe that Points have been earned but not credited, they may be required to submit documentation or other proof satisfactory to Air Arabia, which may include, without limitation to, copies of boarding passes and receipts.
    7. Except for retroactive Point requests for Points earned from eligible Air Arabia flights and eligible ancillary products and services, no retroactive Point requests will be granted. Members must provide their AirRewards account details at the time of the activity in accordance with all instructions, rules and restrictions associated with that particular qualifying activity or purchase offer.
    8. In the event that membership details cannot be attached at the time of purchase for travel sold by travel agents, Members are invited to use an alternate method to register their AirRewards id as described above. Air Arabia reserves the right to make exceptions to this rule at its discretion.
    9. Points will not be awarded on unused, cancelled, forfeited, refunded, credited or fraudulent travel bookings or purchases or activities, as determined by Air Arabia in its sole discretion.
    10. Air Arabia reserves the right to offer incentive bonuses to Members who book travel online through www.airarabia.com.
    11. The activities and/or transactions eligible for earning Points, and/or the amount of Points available to be accrued, are subject to change by Air Arabia, at any time, without notice or liability.
    12. Points cannot be earned on special flights, which may include (but is not a comprehensive list) charter flights, special Hajj/Umrah Flights and repatriation flights.
       
  4. Redemption of AirRewards Points
    1. Members may redeem AirRewards Points for the base fare and surcharge of an Air Arabia flight and/or eligible add-on services on any Air Arabia flight. Members may also use a combination of AirRewards Points and monetary payment for the same. Air Arabia may also offer, in its sole discretion, opportunities to redeem Points for other products or services. Points may not be redeemed for any product or service except as expressly permitted by Air Arabia.
    2. A minimum balance of 3,000 Points is required for a Member to be able to redeem against a flight and baggage reward. The number of Points required is based on the published base fare and surcharge at the time of booking, which depends on, among other things, the flight selection and applicability of peak travel periods. The number of Points required for a particular flight may change at any time until the booking is confirmed and Points are applied as payment. Taxes and fees apply and are the responsibility of the passenger and will vary based on departure and destination cities. Taxes and fees must be paid at the time of flight booking and are subject to change as required by law. Any change to a booking may result in a change to the number of points required and Air Arabia's standard change fee/policy will apply. Any change or cancellation to an Award Flight booking is subject to the Air Arabia standard change and cancellation fees and policies.
    3. Points may be redeemed for Award Travel online at www.airarabia.com/air-rewards-loyalty-program. In order to redeem points for flights online, Members must sign in to their AirRewards account.
    4. Flights which are purchased with a monetary payment may not be exchanged or refunded for payment with Points, in whole or in part.
    5. Air Arabia reserves the right to revise, amend, modify or discontinue the products or services for which Members may use Points, in whole or in part, and/or the amount of Points required to redeem any product or service including, but not limited to, Award Travel, in connection with the Program.
    6. Air Arabia's Flight Terms and Conditions including, but not limited to, change/cancellation policies and fees apply to Award Flights and Ancillary Awards.
    7. Air travel on Air Arabia (including Award Flights) is governed by Air Arabia's Contract of Carriage, a copy of which is available for inspection at http://www.airarabia.com/en/conditions-carriage, Please review these documents for travel requirements and restrictions, liability and baggage limitations and requirements for the travel of minor children. Please visit http://www.airarabia.com/baggage for additional information on baggage requirements and restrictions.
