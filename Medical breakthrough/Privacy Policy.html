Security
Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC continuously reviews its policies and practices to ensure that all customer information remains secure.

You must have cookies enabled in order for you to be able to perform many of the general functions on the Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC web site. With cookies enabled, the Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC web site will have the capacity to deliver you a more customized and complete shopping experience to track visits from our affiliates and partners and to enable the use of our shopping cart. Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC does not use cookies to store or collect any personal information such as names, addresses, or payment information. To learn how to enable cookies on your computer, visit the Web site that corresponds to the browser you use.

Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC collects your IP address, which cannot be used to identify you personally, to help diagnose problems with its server, to administer and tune the operation of its site, and to log your browser type and referring URL information. Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC servers use each IP address to anonymously track the date and duration of each session within its site. Your IP address may also be used to help Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC collect statistical data or in gathering broad usage information. Certain parts of this information may be shared with Medical Breakthrough Massage Chairs LLC partners, but your IP address will not be distributed.
