3.         Provide effective Services to the customer and assist in the event he/she needs additional support; and

4.         Comply with our legal or statutory obligations.

5.         Analyze the app performance, create and serve best products

6.         Send communications, notifications regarding the products, offerings, loans, collections

We do not disclose information about identifiable individuals to other parties, but we may provide them with anonymous aggregate information about our users (for example, we may inform them that 500 men aged under 30 have applied for a loan on any given day).
