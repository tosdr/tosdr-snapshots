Ticket Delivery Methods

Tickets are generally delivered through one of three methods: Print-at-Home/digital delivery, will-call, or postal mail. The delivery options for specific events will be shown during the checkout process.

• Print-at-home tickets are delivered via email to the supplied email address directly after the purchase is complete. Tickets can then be printed by the customer on any standard printer.
• Will-call tickets are not physical tickets. The purchaser’s name, along with any other names specified during checkout, will be delivered to the venue or event promoters for admission to the event. Typically attendees will have to present photo ID to be admitted.
• Postal mail tickets will be sent by U.S. Mail. Unless otherwise specified during checkout and in the receipt, tickets are mailed within 7–10 days of purchase.
