What Is Personally Identifiable Information?

“PII”, “Personally Identifiable Information”, and/or “Personal Information” means any information which may identify an individual, directly or indirectly or, if you are in  the European Economic Area (“EEA”) or United Kingdom (“UK”) any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual. Examples of PII include, but are not limited to, first and last names, home address, billing address or other physical address, email address, telephone number, etc. PII does not include information which is anonymized, and other information which is excluded from the scope of applicable privacy and data protection laws.
