9. Your rights.

The new General Regulation of Data Protection (GDPR for its acronym in English) gives people certain rights related to their personal data. Therefore, with this new privacy policy we inform you in detail and with the greatest transparency of your rights and how to exercise them.

These are your rights in relation to your personal data:
  • Right of access: the right to be informed and request access to personal data that we process about you;
  • Right of rectification: the right to request that we correct or update your personal data, when they are inaccurate or incomplete;
  • Right to delete: the right to request that your personal data be deleted;
  • Right to restrict: the right to request that the processing be stopped temporarily or permanently of all or some of your personal data;
  • Right to object:
    • the processing of your personal data for reasons related to your particular situation;
    • to have your personal data processed for direct marketing purposes;
  • Right to data portability: the right to request a copy of your personal data in electronic format and the right to transmit said data for use in the service of a third party; and
  • The right not to be subject to automated decision making: the right not to be subject to decisions that are based solely on automated decision making, including the creation of profiles, when the decision may have a legal effect on you or produce a similar important effect.
