Our Policy:

Welcome to Upside! Upside Services, Inc. (“Upside”, “we”, “us” and/or “our”) operates the website (https://www.upside.com) (the “Site”), our iOS and Android mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Services”) to our Service visitors (“you”, “your”). This Privacy Policy sets forth Upside’s policy with respect to the collection of personal data (including as defined under privacy rights and consumer data protection laws in various states) (collectively, “Personal Data”) from visitors to the Site and Services.

In addition, your access to and use of the Services is subject to the Terms of Service available here, which may be updated by Upside from time to time.

Our Services are intended for individuals in the United States only.  If you provide Personal Data to the Services, such Personal Data may be transferred from your current location to the offices and servers of Upside and the authorized third parties referred to herein.
‍

Information We Collect:

When you interact with us through the Services, we collect Personal Data and other data from and about you in order to provide the Services (for example, to provide you with the opportunity to earn Cash Back rewards).  

Personal Data That You Provide Through the Services: We collect Personal Data from you when you use the Services and voluntarily provide such information, such as when you create an Upside account or upload an image of a receipt to claim a Cash Back rewards offer. This Personal Data may include:

  • Your email address and password, if you create an Upside account. We use this information to provide you with the Services, including to redeem your offers, communicate with you, and provide you with account security.
  • A unique social media identifier, and possibly your name and/or email address, if you log in to the Upside application using a social media account. We use this information to provide you with account security and to communicate with you.
  • A device identifier associated with your mobile device. We use this information to monitor your account for fraudulent activity.
  • Your history of Upside use, including transaction timestamps, site identifiers, transaction totals, Cash Back rewards earned, and the first six and last four digits of any credit cards used, if you use our Services and generate Cash Back rewards offers. We use this information to provide personalized Cash Back rewards offers to you in the future.
  • Images of your credit card receipts from transactions at Upside merchant partners, if you upload these images to the Services to claim Cash Back rewards offers. Depending on the merchant, such receipts may include additional Personal Data, such your full name or date of birth. We use the transaction information contained on these receipts to verify your claims for Cash Back rewards offers and to update your history of Upside offers. As discussed herein, we engage a third party provider, Amazon Web Services, to provide cloud storage where we store these images.
  • The first six and last four digits of any credit cards or debit cards you input into your Upside wallet, if you choose to use this feature for receiptless transactions. We use this information to verify your claims for Cash Back rewards offers and to enable receiptless check-in at certain partner merchant sites.
  • Geolocation information, if you enable location services on your mobile device. We use this information to verify your claims for Cash Back rewards offers and to notify you when you are near to one of our merchant partners.
  • A record of your touch screen interactions with the user interface for our Services (excluding data that you may input). We use this information to identify design problems with our user interface and to provide a better user experience. 
  • A list of your contacts, including names and email addresses, if you upload your contacts in order to make referrals and generate referral bonuses. In choosing to upload such Personal Data, you represent that you have the authority to share it with Upside and to permit Upside to use it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We use this information to offer our Services to individual contacts you designate, including referral bonuses.
  • The name of your financial institution(s) and the last four digits of your bank account number(s), if you choose to ‘cash out’ your Upside account by receiving a direct transfer to your bank account. We use this information to help distribute Cash Back rewards you have redeemed.
  • Your PayPal login email address, if you choose to ‘cash out’ your Upside account by receiving a direct transfer to your PayPal account. We use this information to help distribute Cash Back rewards you have redeemed.
  • For a small subset of users, we may collect additional categories of Personal Data that those users voluntarily provide to us while using the Services. For example, a user may provide contact information such as an email address, mobile phone number, or mailing address while interacting with customer support or entering an Upside sweepstakes. A user may provide a name, mailing address, and social security number for tax reporting purposes.  A user may provide a state of residence when submitting a privacy request. 
  • In addition to these categories of Personal Data collected by Upside, Upside creates and assigns a unique user identifier (Upside UUID) to every Upside account at the time of creation.

Third Party Personal Data  Processing. For some functionality, Upside relies on third party service providers to collect and process Personal Data from users directly, which may include through use of an application programming interface (API) on the mobile application. 

  • We may require you to provide your phone number for multi-factor authentication, which provides you increased security while using the Services. The multi-factor authentication typically consists of a unique and temporary code sent to you via text message to your phone for purposes of additional authentication on the app. Phone numbers are collected by our third-party partner Okta, Inc. and are not shared with Upside. Okta’s privacy policy is accessible here. Users who wish to exercise their rights regarding Personal Data collected by Okta should contact Okta directly. 
  • Users who add a credit or debit card to their Upside wallets or utilize the Upside Pay feature are prompted to input their name, full credit card or debit card number, expiration date, CVC code, and zip code associated with their billing address.  This information is collected by our third party partner Stripe, Inc., and is not shared with Upside. As outlined above, Upside only collects (through Stripe) the first six and last four digits of any card inputted through Stripe’s API. Stripe uses the Personal Data collected to validate your credit card or debit card and to initiate charges that you authorize. Stripe may also share this Personal Data with our payments processing partners to enable the Upside Pay functionality, initiate and validate changes to your card account, and reconcile the actual purchase amount using Upside Pay. Stripe’s Privacy Policy is accessible here. Users who wish to exercise their rights regarding Personal Data collected by Stripe should contact Stripe directly.
  • Users who ‘cash out’ their Upside account by receiving a direct transfer to their bank accounts are prompted to log in to an online bank portal. This online portal is provided by Plaid, Inc., which shares no information with Upside but provides your full name, email address, the name of your financial institution, and your full bank account number to Dwolla, Inc. As outlined above, Upside only collects (through Dwolla) the name of your financial institution and the last four digits of your bank account number.  Dwolla uses the Personal Data collected to originate credit transfers to your bank account(s) that you select in the mobile application. Plaid’s Privacy Policy is accessible here. Dwolla’s Privacy Policy is accessible here. Users who wish to exercise their rights regarding Personal Data collected by Plaid or Dwolla should contact Plaid or Dwolla directly.

Data We Receive From Merchants.  Merchants who participate on the Upside platform consent to share with Upside anonymous transaction-level data collected from their customers. When you claim a Cash Back rewards offer through Upside, Upside uses Personal Data that you have shared with Upside, such as the time you claimed the offer and your geolocation data, to locate and verify the transaction in the data feed received from the participating merchant and to identify your purchase history with that merchant. By creating an Upside account and using the Services, you authorize Upside to use your Personal Data to perform this data matching and identify your purchase history.
