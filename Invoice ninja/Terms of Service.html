Kanban Boards to Manage Projects

Forever Free

A Kanban board is a project management tool which provides a visualization of single or multiple tasks & workflows. It’s a tool based on a work philosophy originating in Japan which emphasizes focus on one particular task at a time over a multitask approach, as well as the benefits of collaboration.

Kanban boards are designed to make planning, managing, and completing tasks easier thanks to a visual overview of the entire work process. You get to see what needs to be done, when it needs to be done, who needs to do it, and how much is left to do, all on one color-coded board.

Recurring Invoicing & Auto-Billing Clients

Forever Free

If you have clients that need to be billed again & again on a fixed schedule (weekly, monthly, etc…), Invoice Ninja offers auto-billing via the world’s favorite payment gateways:

  • Stripe
  • WePay
  • Braintree
  • Authorize.net​​
  • Mollie
  • GoCardless
  • Square
  • Eway Rapid
  • PayFast
  • Checkout.com
  • Paytrace
  • Forte

Auto-billing (AKA “token billing”) is a feature that enables the payments system to capture your clients’ credit card details and store them for billing at a future date. Token billing enables auto-billing, which means future invoices can be issued automatically. Invoice Ninja integrates seamlessly so you can enjoy all the advantages of token billing via your Invoice Ninja account.
