Based on permission, Pacogames collects the following types of information.

1.1 Information collected within a legitimate interest

1.1.1 Analytic Tools
  • We use third-party analytics tools to help us measure traffic and usage trends for the Service. These tools collect information sent by your device or our Service, including the web pages you visit, add-ons, and other information that assists us in improving the Service. We collect and use this analytics information with analytics information from other Users so that it cannot reasonably be used to identify any particular individual User.

  • To improve the user's experience, PacoGames uses browser storage in the form of cookies and local storages. An example of the saved data can be the list of games played in the given browser, or recorded scores in the played games. This data is stored only on the client side and does not serve to identify a particular user.

By consensus, the user agrees with extension of the authorization to collect and process data with the following additions. The validity period of the consent is 3 years, if the user does not withdraw his consent earlier. Consent also expires by deleting the user's account.

  • By consensus, the user extends the collection of the data mentioned in section 1.1.1 about advanced measurement tools for personalized PacoGames content recommendations. This content recommendation can take place through both the web and the ad network.

  • Login identifiers. This may be the email address and password, or any data provided by the third party through which the login occurred (Facebook, Google+, VK, GameArter).

  • Age of the user for the purpose of verifying compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA).

  • Devices used by the user and the last access IP address. These information allow for better security of user accounts against unauthorized logins.
  • Preferred content for the user based on his behavior and activity on the website and in the games.
1.2.3 Device Identifiers
  • A device identifier may deliver information to us or to a third party partner about how you browse and use the Service and may help us or others provide reports or personalized content and ads. Some features of the Service may not function properly if use or availability of device identifiers is impaired or disabled.

Communication made over contact form, bug report, email, telephone, post or different offline supported way is storred for a time necessary for its execution. (maximum of 3 weeks, unless otherwise provided by the law). Using the bug report form, the user automatically sends his browser and operating system signature.
