2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND HOW WE COLLECT IT

In the following we will tell you which types of personal data we collect about you and how we collect it. In section 3 we have a table explaining the purposes for which we process your personal data and the lawful basis we rely on.

We collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

In most situations the information is collected directly from you when you visit our website, register to use our services, request marketing from us or otherwise communicate with us. We also receive information from third party sources.

We will only apply cookies -- other than strictly necessary technical cookies - if you have provided your consent. Please see our Cookie Notice for further details.

We combine the personal data we receive from other sources with personal data we collect from you (or your device) and use it as described in this Privacy Policy.

8. HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS

You have certain choices available to you when it comes to your personal data. Below is a summary of those choices, how to exercise them and any limitations.

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data. This enables you to receive a copy of the personal data we hold about you and to check that we are lawfully processing it. Please note that there may be circumstances in which the personal data cannot be disclosed, e.g. because of restrictions in the law.

  • Request rectification of the personal data that we hold about you. This enables you to have any incomplete or inaccurate information we hold about you corrected.

  • Request erasure of your personal data (the right to be forgotten). This enables you to ask us to delete or remove your personal data. Please note that there may be circumstances in which the personal data cannot be deleted, e.g. because of restrictions in the law.

  • Object to processing of your personal data at any time.

  • Receive your personal data provided to us as a data controller in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and to transmit that personal data to another controller (data portability).

  • Where our processing is solely based on your specific consent you have the right to with-draw your consent at any time. Such withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

If you wish to exercise any of the data protection rights that are available to you then please send your request to us by email at legal@flowplayer.com and we will action your request in accordance with applicable data protection laws. For your protection, we will only implement requests with respect to the personal data associated with the particular email address that you use to send us your request, and we may need to verify your identity before implementing your request.

You can also review, correct and update certain information that you provided by logging into your Service account and editing your profile information.

You have the right to complain to your local data protection authority if you are unhappy with our data protection practices. In Sweden, you can lodge a complaint with Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten / IMY.
