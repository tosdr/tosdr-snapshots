When you use the Services or engage in certain activities, such as registering for an Account with us, posting on our Services, responding to surveys, requesting Services or information, or contacting us directly, we may ask you to provide some or all of the following types of information:


      2. Account Information. If you create an Account using your login credentials using a SNS Account, we may be able to access and collect your name, email address and other information (such as unique user ID, tokens, gender and date of birth) that your privacy settings and/or the privacy policy on the applicable SNS Account may permit us to access.


      3. Account. You may use certain features of the Services even without an Account (such as viewing pre-recorded videos). If you want to use certain features of the Services (such as posting a comment to a video or giving a virtual gift to another user), however, you will need to have an Account. There are two types of Accounts: (i) third party accounts based on users’ SNS

        Account; and (ii) email Account. Some features of our Services are not available to Accounts based on SNS Accounts or Email Accounts options.


      4. Creating an Account. Creating an Account may require you to provide your cell phone number for verification. Some features of our Services are restricted for Accounts based on SNS Accounts or Email Accounts. If you choose the SNS Account option, we will obtain from your existing SNS Account certain information such as your user name, profile photo, and other personal information we may obtain (but to the extent that your SNS Account privacy settings permit us to access such information).

      6. Communications with Us. We may collect Personal Information from you such as name, phone number, email address, or mailing address when you choose to request information about our Services, request to receive customer or technical support, or otherwise communicate with us.


      7. Surveys. We may contact you to participate in surveys. If you do decide to participate, you may be asked to provide certain information which may include Personal Information. All information collected from your participation in our surveys is provided by you voluntarily.


      8. Interactive Features. Our Services contain interactive functionality that allows you to engage with other users on the Services, vote, provide feedback, post comments to our forums, live stream yourself and your surroundings using your device’s camera, upload videos, photographs and other content, participate in surveys, and otherwise interact with the Services and with other users. If you use any interactive functionality on our Services that requests or permits you to provide us with Personal Information (including, for example, any Services that allow you to post User Content), we collect the Personal Information that you provide to us in the course of using these interactive features. If you choose to submit content to any public area of the Service, such content will be considered “public” and will not be subject to the privacy protections set forth herein. Please be aware that we have little or no control over how Individuals may use the information you share through our interactive features.


      9. Chat Functionality. Each Individual with an Account may have access to their own personal chatroom. Other users may freely join your chat room and post messages. Any messages posted in your personal chatroom are visible to anyone who joined the chatroom prior to you posting the message. Your communication in chatrooms is not private and you should assume any communications you make in such chatrooms is made publicly available to any other person. You should exercise caution before engaging in any chatroom communications and before joining any chatrooms and never share any of your information with others via the Services, including any Personal Information and other identifiers. Please be aware that we have little or no control over how Individuals may use the information you share in chatrooms.


      10. Automatic Data Collection. We may collect certain information automatically when you use the Services. This information may include your Internet protocol (IP) address, user settings, IMEI, MAC address, cookie identifiers, mobile carrier, mobile advertising and other unique identifiers, details about your browser, operating system or device, location information,

        Internet service provider, pages that you visit before, during and after using the Services, information about the links you click, and other information about how you use the Services. Information we collect may be associated with accounts and other devices.


      11. Information from Other Sources. We may receive information about you from other sources, including through third party services and organizations to supplement information provided by you. This supplemental information allows us to verify information that you have provided to us and to enhance our ability to provide you with information about our business, products, and Services.


      12. Video Identifiers. The Service’s functionality will assign a unique series of numbers and letters preceded by a hashtag, octothorpe or pound sign (i.e. “#”) to identify each video available through the Services. The Services may use these video identifiers to track and associate your activities to the videos you watched or donated to. For example, if you give a virtual gift to a video, the gift may be shown publicly and be shown tied to the specific video and the user you gave the gift to.

        • Generally manage Individual information and accounts;

        Site or the App. The use of a pixel allows us to record, for example, that a user has visited a particular web page or clicked on a particular advertisement.
