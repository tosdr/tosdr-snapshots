Introduction

Evasyst , Inc. d/b/a Kast ( “Company,” “We,” “Us,” “Our”), a Delaware corporation, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this privacy policy (this “Policy”).

This Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit any of the Company’s website domains such as www.kastapp.co or kast.gg (collectively our “Website”) or download, install, register with, access or use the Company’s desktop, web, or mobile applications (each, an “ App”, collectively the “ Apps”), and access or use the services offered by the Company through the Website and the Apps (collectively referred to as the “Services”), and describes our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

The purpose of this Policy is to further provide you with information about the processing of your Personal Information in accordance with the information obligations set out in the EU General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR).

This Policy applies to information that we collect:

  • On the Website or through the use of an App.
  • In email, text, and other electronic messages between us and you and this Website or in conjunction with using our Services.

It does not apply to information collected by:

  • Use offline or through any other means, including on any other websites operated by any third party; or
  • Any third party, including through any website, application or content (including advertising) that may be linked to or be accessible from the Website, any App or through your use of the Services.

Please read this Policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Website. Personal Information is defined below in the Section that has the title “Information We Collect About You and How We Collect It.” This Policy may change from time to time. For information about our policies regarding changes to the Policy, see the Section that has the title “Changes to Our Privacy Policy” below. Please check the Policy periodically for updates. We will not make substantial changes to this Policy or reduce your rights under this Policy without providing a notice thereof.

Personal Information of Children

Our Website and Apps are not intended for children under 13 years of age (or up to 16 years of age in certain EU jurisdictions as set out in our Terms of Service). No one under such approved age may provide any information to the Website or through use of an App. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under the approved age. If you are under the approved age, do not use this Website, any App or the Services or provide any information on or through them, including through on or through any of the features on the Website or App. No one under the approved age may register on the Website or through any App, use any of the interactive or public comment features of this Website or any App or provide any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, email address, or any screen name or user name you may use. If we learn that we have collected or received Personal Information from a child under the approved age without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under the approved age, please contact us at support@kastapp.co

Data Controller Information

Evasyst, Inc. d/b/a Kast makes decisions about how your information will be used. Our contact information is: Evasyst, Inc., Attn: Evasyst Privacy , 340 S Lemon Ave #9980 Walnut, CA 91789. You can also contact us by email at support@kastapp.co.

Information We Collect About You and How We Collect It

We generally collect several types of information from and about users of our Website and Apps and users who communicate with us, including information:

  • By which you may be personally identified, such as your vanity user name, e-mail address, profile pictures, or any other information that we collect through the Website or Services that is defined as personal or personally identifiable information under any applicable law (“ Personal Information ”).
  • Required for us to identify and authenticate you and secure your interactions with the Website and the Apps.
  • That is about you but individually does not identify you. For instance, we collect information about your self-reported interests.
  • About your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our Website and Services and usage details.

We collect this information:

  • Directly from you when you provide it to us.
  • Automatically as you navigate through the Website, Apps or use our Services. Information collected automatically may include information on your usage details of the Website, Apps or Service, your computer or digital device’s ID or IP addresses address(that is, a unique string of numbers that identifies each device using the Internet Protocol to communicate over the internet), and information collected through cookies, pixels and web beacons (we describe what these are in the section of this Privacy Policy captioned “Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies.”).

Information You Provide to Us. The information we collect that you provide to us on or through our Website or your use of the Apps and Service may include:

  • Information that you provide by filling in forms on our Website or on an App or when using our Services. This includes information provided at the time of registering (including your email address, your username and your password). We may also ask you for information when you contact us through our Website or through an App, including your email address and your phone number.
  • Information that you can provide when you set up your profile, including your interests, and location.
  • Information that you upload, post, cast or stream, which may include information, writings, files, videos, images or photographs of you or others.
  • When you subscribe to the Services or make other purchases, you must provide information to Our third party payment processor supporting a valid payment method, including credit card information. This information will include accurate and complete billing information including your full name, address, state, zip code, telephone number, and valid payment method information.
  • Metadata from files that you upload, post, cast or stream to the Website, through an App or to (or by means of) our Services.
  • Records and copies of your correspondence (including email addresses), if you contact us.
  • Information about any connections that you make with other users through use of the Services.
  • Information that you submit through our Website and Services when you correspond with us and when you correspond with other users, including through our chat, posting and messaging features.
  • Information that you provide to us when you call us or communicate with us by electronic mail.
  • Usernames and passwords that we use to authenticate your access to the Website, the Apps and the Services.

Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies. As you navigate through and interact with our Website, our Apps or use our Services we may also use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns, including:

  • Details of your visits to our Website, Apps and our Services, including traffic data, access times, accessed pages, location data, logs, and other communication data and the resources that you access and use on the Website or Apps.
  • Details of whether or not our users have opened our emails, although we only use this data to know track the aggregate number of users who have opened our emails, not whether any particular user opened a specific email.
  • Information about your computer and internet connection, including your IP address, domain name, device ID, operating system, browser type and language, and other unique identifying information, including information of machines that have our software downloaded and installed on them.

The information we collect automatically may include Personal Information, or and we may maintain it or associate it with Personal Information we collect in other ways or receive from third parties. Such collection and association helps us to improve our Website, Apps and the Services offered and to deliver a better and more personalized service, including by enabling us to:

  • Estimate our audience size and usage patterns.
  • Store information about your preferences, allowing us to customize our Website, and Apps and Service according to your individual interests.
  • Speed up your searches.
  • Recognize you when you return to our Website.
  • Identify you and ensure the security of our Website, the Apps and the Services and the transmission and storage of data between you and us.
  • Control unauthorized use or abuse of our Website, the Apps and our Services.

The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:

  • Cookies (or browser cookies). A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. You can use your browser settings to instruct your browser to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of our Website, the Apps or use certain Services. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you direct your browser to our Website. You can still review the Website if you choose to set your browser to refuse all cookies; however, session cookies must be enabled to establish an account and to download and install any of our software.
  • Web Beacons. Pages of our the Website, the Apps and our emails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit the Company, for example, to count users who have visited those pages or opened an email and for other related website statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain website content and verifying system and server integrity).

Personal Information is not collected by us via cookies and other tracking technologies. If you have previously provided Personal Information to us, however, cookies may be tied, linked or associated to such information. We do not collect real-time information through the Website, any App or the Services about the location of your device.

Other Tracking Technologies

Our Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, to understand how our customers interact with our Website. The information generated by the cookie about your use of our website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on its servers in the United States or around the world. Google will use this information for analyzing your use of our website, generating reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage.

It is possible to opt-out of Google Analytics with the following browser add-on tool: Google Analytics opt-out add-on.
