Usage Data is data collected automatically either generated by the use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself (for example, the duration of a page visit).

Data Processor (or Service Providers)

We may use the services of various Service Providers in order to process your data more effectively.

Data Subject

Data Subject is any living individual who is the subject of Personal Data.

Client

The Client is the individual using our Service. The Client corresponds to the Data Subject, who is the subject of Personal Data.

User

The User is the individual, listed in Clients sub-account and can be employee, agent orrepresentative of a Client, who primarily uses the restricted areas of client area for the purpose of accessing the Service.The User corresponds to the Data Subject, who is the subject of Personal Data.

Contact

The Contact is the individual, listed in Clients sub-account and has no access to our systems. It is primarily used for purposes of providing Personal Data to the other service providers(like domain registrars). The Contact corresponds to the Data Subject, who is the subject of Personal Data.

Visitor

The Visitor is the individual other than a User, who uses our websites, but has no access to restricted areas (where access requires logging in).
