Code of Conduct

At Grey Arrows Drone Club, we are committed to fostering a welcoming and responsible community of drone enthusiasts. Our goal is to promote safe and enjoyable drone flying experiences. To achieve this, we require all Associates, Members and participants to adhere to the following Code of Conduct:

  1. Safety First:
    Prioritise safety at all times during drone operations.
    Comply with all applicable drone laws and regulations set forth by the UK Civil Aviation Authority (CAA) and any local authorities.

  2. Respect for Others:
    Treat all club Associates, Members, visitors, and the public with courtesy and respect.
    Refrain from engaging in any form of harassment, discrimination, or offensive behaviour based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, or any other personal characteristic.

  3. Responsible Flying:
    Check Drone Scene before flying for flight restrictions and NOTAMS.
    Maintain visual contact with your drone at all times and avoid flying in restricted airspace or near airports unless authorised to do so.
    Do not fly drones over large events, or near emergency situations.

  4. Environmental Awareness:
    Fly drones with consideration for the environment and wildlife.
    Pick up any litter or debris left behind after flying.

  5. Adherence to Rules:
    Comply with all Club rules included in the FAQ and in the Terms of Service.
    Follow the instructions of Club and Meet-up organisers during drone-related events.

  6. Knowledge Sharing:
    Encourage knowledge sharing and support fellow members in learning about drone technology, regulations, and safe flying practices.

  7. Reporting Concerns:
    Report any incidents or safety concerns promptly to the Club Committee.
    Inform the Club Committee of any violations of this Code of Conduct.

By adhering to this Code of Conduct we can create a thriving drone community where safety, respect, and responsible flying are upheld.

Failure to comply with the Code of Conduct will result in sanctions, including the revocation of membership and participation rights.

Competitions and Prize Draws

Competitions held by Grey Arrows Drone Club are skills-based and require a question to be answered. Entrants who successfully answer a question are entered in to a prize draw. Grey Arrows Drone Club has a reasonable expectation that skill, judgment or knowledge of the prize is required to enter the competition. Entry to the Competition is only available to Club members residing in the UK with an active membership subscription. Non-members and non-UK residents are not eligible to enter.

Entry to a Competition can only be made online via https://dronescene.co.uk/competitions and ticket purchases can only be made using a debit or credit card.

Some Competitions are limited to one ticket purchase, other competitions have no limit on the number of tickets you may purchase. A maximum of 99 tickets can be purchased in any one transaction. Multiple transactions of ticket purchases are allowed where the Competition permits.

Each Competition has a Start Date, an End Date and a Draw Date. Tickets may only be purchased during the period between the Start Date and the End Date of the Competition.

Prizes are non-transferable and no cash alternative prize is available.

Entrants who successfully answer a question will have their ticket(s) entered in to a prize draw to choose a winner. The more tickets purchased by an individual, the higher the odds of winning the prize draw. The prize draw will be held live, online. A link to the live draw event will be made available after the End Date and the link will remain active until the draw has been completed. A live-chat will also be available during the live draw, the link to which will also be made available at the time. Entrants who answer the competition question incorrectly will not have their tickets entered in to the prize draw. Ticket purchases are non-refundable.

Winners will be notified by telephone call and email within 2 days of the Draw Date. You must ensure your contact details are up to date on your account. Our Privacy Policy is available at: https://greyarro.ws/privacy

Prizes must be claimed within 14 days of the Draw Date. Unclaimed prizes will be re-drawn at a future date and offered to an alternative entrant of the same draw. Prizes will be dispatched for delivery via courier within 7 to 14 days of the Draw Date.

Competitions winners will be invited to take part in a publicity opportunity. This is optional and is at the winners discretion. Grey Arrows Drone Club will only refer to and reference the winner using their publicly visible username. The winners real name and any other personal information will not be disclosed. A list of previous winners is available for participants to view.
