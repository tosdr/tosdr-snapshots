Your Privacy and Security

Prime Life Fibers Inc and the Wearever® brand understands the concerns of online shoppers. We are committed to the highest standards of privacy and security.

We are a secure shopping site and use the latest and best technology to assure you the greatest protection.

Wearever® respects the privacy of our users. Under no circumstances will we sell, share, or rent user's private information for profit. We collect information from our users at several different points on our website in order to fulfill the direct requests and orders from our users.
