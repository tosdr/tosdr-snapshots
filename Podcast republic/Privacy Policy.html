    Privacy

    This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on Podcast Repulic’s website and our apps for Android and any other devices and platforms.

    Information we collect

    When creating an account, you will be asked to enter an optional email address and a password, or through Google login service. Email addresses are only used for logging in, password resets, responding to emails that you initiate, and notifications that you request. We don’t send promotional emails.

    When rating a podcast or leaving a review for a podcast, an unique device ID will be stored to track the review for editing/deleting purposes. This unique device is also needed to combat fraud and abuse. This unique device ID won't be shared with any third parties.

    We store information about your podcasts, radio stations, episodes, and listening progress to sync this information between the website and your devices. We also collect anonymous statistics about which podcasts are most popular to help inform our recommendation engine.

    The Podcast Republic’s Android app uses third party services that may collect inforaction used to idenfity you. Main third party services used includes Google Play services, YouTube Data API, etc.

    Information usage

    We use the information we collect to operate and improve our website, apps, and customer support.

    We do not share personal information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to accomplish Podcast Repulic functionality. We may share anonymous, aggregate statistics with outside parties, such as how many people listen to a particular podcast with Podcast Repulic.

    We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.

     

    Log Data

    We want to inform you that whenever you use Podcast Republic’s Android app, in a case of an error in the app we may collect data and information on your device called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, information about your podcasts, radio stations, episodes, and listening progress and other statistics. The Log Data will be stored on your device. The Log Data will not be shared with any kind of third party service without your consent.

These have their own independent privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.
