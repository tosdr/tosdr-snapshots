  • "Smart Greetings", which allows customized greetings depending on who is calling you and automatically greets your callers with both your name and their name. Smart Greetings can be overridden by your selection of a greeting that you or someone else has recorded.
  • "Auto replies", which under certain circumstances (a) provides callers a text message confirming to callers that they have left a voicemail message successfully, (b) may include a custom message, and (c) permits callers to see and modify the information shown with respect to their caller ID. You can use a YouMail setting to control this behavior, and you can allow list individual phone numbers to ensure that such numbers are never blocked or put into a spam folder when they call you.
