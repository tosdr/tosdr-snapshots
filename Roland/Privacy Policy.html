About The Protection of Personal Information (Privacy Policy)

Roland Corporation recognizes the importance of protecting the privacy of all personal information provided by users of this website. We created the following policy guidelines with fundamental respect for our users’ right to privacy and because we value our relationships with our users.

Roland Corporation (hereinafter referred to as “we”) and its group companies may ask you to register your personal information, including your name, address, telephone number, and e-mail address, when we conduct online surveys, you sign up for membership sites, we distribute e-mail newsletters, you submit applications for events and contests, you wish to register products and inquire about product support. We recognize that it is our social responsibility to protect such personal information.

We retain “personal data” (also called “personal information” or “personally identifiable information”) covered by this Privacy Policy mainly on servers located in Japan and the U.S. Please note that this Privacy Policy is exclusively governed by Japanese law.

Roland Corporation takes reasonable precautions to keep the personal information disclosed to us secure. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, technical, electronic and managerial security procedures to safeguard and secure the personal information we collect online from our users. Nevertheless, Roland Corporation does not guarantee the security, accuracy or completeness of any such information or procedures. Our protection of personal information is based on the following principles. Please also read our Policies on the Protection of Personal Information.

1. We use it only for the stated purposes

We always limit our use of personal information to within the purposes stated below. If we need to use personal information for other purposes, we will inform you in advance. If you do not agree to the new purposes, you can deny permission for us to use your personal information for such purposes.

Purposes of use

  1. Provision of membership sites
    • To identify the membership registration applicant, members, and members service (hereinafter referred to as “Service”) users
    • To authenticate the user when using the Service
  2. Provision of a variety of information by e-mail and direct mail
    • To offer information related to products and services of the Company and group companies including promotion and advertising and event information
    • To offer information including promotion, advertising and event information related to products and services of third parties that have been engaged by the Company
    • To notify and inform registered users about other information
  3. Customer Support
    • To receive inquiries from, consult with and correspond with users
    • To provide after-sales services, such as version updates, to users
    • To dispatch repaired products back to users
  4. Marketing (to plan, develop and improve products and services)
    • To understand user registration status and usage by members on membership sites
    • To conduct questionnaires/surveys and monitor surveys
  5. Other
    • To notify changes of the purposes of use of the personal information and obtain consent
    • Otherwise, to be used pursuant to consent separately obtained from the member

2. We will not disclose personal information to third parties

We will not disclose the personal information you submit to us to any third party except as provided below.

  • When you agree to such disclosure
  • When we, under strict controls, disclose personal information to such companies with which we have an agreement to delegate the processing of personal information
  • When it is considered appropriate in order to enable a group companies or distributor to respond to your inquiry
  • When we are required to disclose or provide personal information by law or regulation
  • When disclosure is needed to protect human health or safety, or assets, and it is difficult to obtain your prior consent quickly
  • When we need to cooperate with national or local governments, and obtaining your consent may hinder those efforts
  • When we use personal information with group companies as described in section 4.

3. We will not transfer personal information overseas

We will not transfer personal information you submit to us to an overseas location, except as provided below.

  • When you agree to such transfer
  • If the recipient jurisdiction is recognized by the rules of Japan’s Personal Information Protection Committee as being a country that has an equivalent level of protection of personal information as Japan, in order to protect the rights and interests of individuals
  • When providing the personal information to any person/entity who has established safeguards/protocols that comply with the standards set forth under the rules of the Personal Information Protection Committee as adequate, on a continuous basis, to the measures which should be taken by a business operator handling personal information. Group companies referred to as recipient of personal information as described in section 4, comply with such standards.
  • When we are required to disclose or provide the personal information by law or regulation
  • When disclosure is needed to protect human health or safety, or assets, and it is difficult to obtain your prior consent
  • When we need to cooperate with national or local governments and obtaining your consent may hinder those efforts

5. We take necessary and appropriate safety measures

We will take necessary and appropriate safety measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage to personal information. We also conduct necessary and appropriate supervision of employees and contractors (including subcontractors) handling personal information. Our company regulations describe specific safety measures to ensure the safe processing of personal information, including the followings.

Establish rules on handling of personal information

  • Company regulations set the handling methods, responsible party, employee in charge, and their duties at each stage of acquisition, use, storage, provision, deletion, and disposal of personal information.

Organizational safety measures

  • Each department that handles personal information has a manager who is responsible for protecting personal information, and work to ensure proper management.
  • We clarify the scope of personal information handled by employees, and have established a system to report to the responsible party and related departments in the event that any fact or sign of violation of the Personal Information Protection Law or company regulations is identified.
  • We conduct periodic self-inspections of the handling of personal information and conduct audits by other departments.

Human resource safety measures

  • We regularly train our employees on matters of concern regarding the handling of personal data.
  • Company regulations set matters concerning confidentiality of personal information.

Physical safety measures

  • Personal information stored in documents, electronic media, etc. is appropriately managed in accordance with company regulations on personal information protection, document management, and information system security.

Technical safety measures

  • Access control is implemented to limit employees who have access to personal information and how much personal information they have access to.

Understanding external environment

  • Some of the personal information we collect is stored on servers in the United States and Ireland. We understand rules on protection of personal information in these countries and implement safety measures.

You can reject cookies from being stored on your computer via your computer’s browser settings, but please be aware that, in such case, certain functions of our website may be impaired or unavailable to you.

In addition, in order to deliver the most relevant advertising to customers, we use behavioral targeting advertising services provided by the following companies using cookies.
