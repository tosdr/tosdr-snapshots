Welcome to Kumu!

Kumu Privacy Policy (v3.0)At kumu, as part of our commitment to create a safe space, we are working our best to ensure that your privacy is respected and your Personal Data is protected. 

Thus, we want you to take a moment and understand what data we obtain about you and our data privacy practices and policies, so you can make an informed decision and exercise meaningful control over your data.

HIGHLIGHTS 

You should read our Privacy Policy in its entirety, but here are a few key points we hope you will take away:

  • kumu is accessible to the general public from anywhere in the world except in a few countries where the service is unavailable in order for kumu to comply with the law or its company policy.
  • When you livestream, create a profile, follow a streamer, give virtual gifts, participate in campaigns or post a clip on kumu that are public or intended to be public in nature, individuals that use kumu will access this information and may have the ability to acquire, use, or reveal such information to others. Furthermore, several of our Services (as defined below) are intended to offer others with information on user behavior, such as virtual gifting or a follow to a streamer. We advise you to keep this in mind when using kumu. However, we also provide non-public communication channels on kumu, such as private streaming and chat.
  • When you participate in campaigns sponsored by our Business Partners (i.e. any company or individual which transacts business with Kumu, including but not limited to advertisers and merchants), we may share your Personal Data with them in accordance with the terms and conditions of the campaigns. For example, if you participate in DTI (Philippines) registered campaigns of a Business Partner, we may share your information with DTI (Philippines) as part our legal obligation and with the Business Partner as part of our contractual obligation.
  • When you purchase products from our Business Partners, we may share your Personal Data with them to fulfill your orders and to third party service providers for the delivery of your ordered products.
  • We collect certain Personal Data (e.g. information regarding the device you’re using, IP address, etc.) from you when you use kumu, even if you’re simply browsing livestreams. We use this information to keep the platform safe and to offer you more relevant content, suggested users and advertisements.
  • We want to hear from you if you have any concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, how we collect or use your Personal Data, or anything else relating to our policies or practices at kumu. You are welcome to contact us at any time using the contact details set out at the end of the Privacy Policy.

Right to appeal

  • Right to data portability – to receive certain Personal Data that you have provided to us, in a machine-readable form and/or that we transmit it to a third party with your express authorisation; and
  • Right to lodge a complaint – to complain to the relevant supervisory authority if your privacy rights are violated, or if you have suffered as a result of unlawful processing of your Personal Data.
