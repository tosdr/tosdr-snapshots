Who Can Join?

How Does It Work?

Becoming a Member.

You can sign up to become a Member (1) at a participating Abercrombie & Fitch or abercrombie kids store; (2) through our UK Website (www.Abercrombie.com); or (3) through our UK Mobile Application ("App"). Upon signing up to become a Member, you will receive an email confirming your Membership in myAbercrombie ("Membership"). Only one (1) myAbercrombie Membership is permitted per person.

Membership and participation is issued free of charge (subject to possible internet connection charges to complete the registration and to access your Membership via the UK Website and the UK App, and such costs are borne by you according to your telephone operator or ISP's tariffs).

Registering Purchases.

When making a purchase in a store, Members may register purchases with their myAbercrombie Membership at checkout by having the associate scan the myAbercrombie ID found inside the Member's myAbercrombie account on the UK App. Associates can also look up a Member's myAbercrombie ID using the email address or telephone number provided upon registration to be a Member of myAbercrombie. When making purchases online, Members must sign into their myAbercrombie account prior to making the purchase. Note, all purchases made when using the myAbercrombie ID (both in store and online) are available to be viewed on the "My Orders" and "History" sections in your myAbercrombie Membership.

Earning Points

Points are earned at the time of in-store purchase when a Member provides or scans their myAbercrombie ID at stores or if a Member is signed into their Membership. When ordering online, points will be earned at the time of shipment. Only one (1) myAbercrombie ID can be used per transaction.

Members earn 10 points for every net GBP spent (including using gift cards as tender on purchases) on any Eligible Product Purchases (as defined below). Net GBP spent is the amount spent on an Eligible Product Purchase, excluding the use of merchandise credit as tender and minus the costs specified below on point calculations.

Points earned will be rounded to the nearest whole GBP.

Point calculations are based upon GBP spent at checkout on Eligible Product Purchases. "Eligible Product Purchases" means purchases of Abercrombie & Fitch and abercrombie kids products such as clothing, accessories, and personal care items such as fragrance. The following costs are excluded from point calculation of Eligible Product Purchases: shipping & handling or delivery charges, gift wrap fees, and purchase of gift cards or e-gift cards. Also, Members do not earn points for the amount of any coupon, discounts, or merchandise credit applied to purchases, as well as the value of any Reward being redeemed. A&F has the right to make the final decision on whether any product purchase qualifies as an Eligible Product Purchase for points.

Points will post to the corresponding myAbercrombie Membership within approximately forty-eight hours (48) hours from in-store purchase or date of online or store order shipment. If Members do not identify themselves at time of purchase, a manual adjustment can be made to add the points to the myAbercrombie Membership for up to thirty (30) days from the actual purchase date, but no purchases prior to the launch of myAbercrombie can be added. Please see the "History" section of Membership for instructions on how to do this.

Points are also earned for approved online reviews on the product page(s) of the item(s) you purchased. You may review a product or products from a post purchase email you receive after placing an order. For each approved review, you will receive two hundred (200) points. It can take up to ten (10) days for the points to post to the corresponding myAbercrombie Membership.

myAbercrombie may identify additional opportunities for Members to earn points from time to time. For example, those tasks may include the following:

  • Sharing additional optional details in your Member profile. Points may be awarded for each additional detail provided with a Membership profile (details which qualify to earn points will be indicated within myAbercrombie, but will not include name or email address as these are required files for Membership creation). These points will only be awarded once per lifetime of each Membership.
  • Initial Membership sign-in on App. Points may be awarded for the initial sign-in to myAbercrombie on the App after the first App download. Points will only be awarded once per lifetime of each Membership.
  • In-store check-in. myAbercrombie Members are eligible to earn 100 points by checking in through the A&F App during store visits. This offer is not valid outside the US, EU, and UK. myAbercrombie Members are eligible to redeem offer one (1) time per day and up to fifty-two (52) times per Calendar Year. A&F associates are not eligible to receive points for in-store check-ins.

When Members return products to the store or return online purchases by mail, points previously posted for that purchase will be deducted from the point balance of the myAbercrombie Membership provided at the time of purchase. If a Member's point balance is insufficient to cover a deduction related to a return, the Member's point balance may go into the negative, and any Membership reaching into a negative balance is subject to be suspended from all further activity at the discretion of A&F. Members with a suspended account will need to reach A&F's Customer Service Department to seek reinstatement. If an account has a negative balance, all earned points will apply first to the negative balance before a Member may begin earning points eligible for conversion to a Reward.

A&F reserves the right to cancel points where it reasonably suspects abuse, fraud and/or errors in the recording of awarded points. If you have questions about the cancelation of points please email us at Abercrombie@Abercrombie.com or call us at 0808 234 0796 or at +1-925-359-2579.

Points are property of A&F, have no cash value, and are not transferable to anyone else or any other Membership in any capacity for any reason. Unused points will expire twelve (12) months after they have been issued if there is no activity on the Membership within the twelve (12) month period. Points that remain on a myAbercrombie Membership will also expire and are forfeited if your myAbercrombie Membership is closed. Points may not be combined among Members.

VIP Qualification

myAbercrombie Members will qualify for VIP status when they spend £700 in a Member Year. "Member Year" is defined as twelve (12) months from the date of the Member's first registration and each anniversary thereafter, for the duration of a Membership. VIP status is good for the Member Year in which you qualify, and the following Member Year. To maintain your VIP status, you must re-qualify every Member Year. In the event you return a purchase which qualified you for VIP status, the point value of the return will be deducted from your Membership account and you could lose your VIP status.

VIP Perks

VIP myAbercrombie Members earn free standard shipping on all orders. In addition to free standard shipping, VIPs can receive occasional VIP exclusive offers.

Rewards cannot be used for purchases of gift cards, e-gift cards, shipping & handling or delivery charges, and gift wrap. Rewards are applied pre-VAT, and cannot be applied to previous purchases. Rewards will be posted to myAbercrombie Memberships within approximately twenty-four (24) hours after points are converted into a Reward. Rewards should be available for use soon after Member has received Rewards. Once points have been converted into a Reward, they cannot be converted back into points even if the Reward is never redeemed and expired (except as described below).

Members may earn up to a maximum of one hundred thousand (100,000) points per calendar year. "Calendar Year" is defined as the one-year period beginning on January 1 and ending on December 31. Therefore, as an example, Members in good standing may earn up to a maximum of forty (40) £10 Rewards per Calendar Year, twenty (20) £20 Rewards per Calendar Year, roughly thirteen (13) £30 Rewards per Calendar Year, roughly ten (10) £40 Rewards per Calendar Year, or other various combinations of different Rewards values.

Rewards are property of A&F, have no cash value, and are not transferable to anyone else or any other Membership in any capacity for any reason.
