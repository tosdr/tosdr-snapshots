PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 12/5/2023

INTRODUCTION

Bound Tree Medical (Bound Tree, we, us, our) is committed to respecting your privacy and protecting the information you provide to us. This privacy notice (Notice) applies to personal information about you obtained through www.boundtree.com (the Website), via emails sent to us at email addresses ending in @boundtree.com, all personal information provided to us in connection with applications for employment, all personal information we may collect when you enter our premises and all other personal information that we may collect from you directly.

This Notice describes how we may collect, use, and disclose such information; the choices available to you regarding our collection, use and disclosure of your information; how we safeguard the information we collect; and how to contact us with any questions regarding our privacy practices. By accessing or otherwise using any part of the Website, you agree to the collection, use, disclosure practices and other activities as described in this policy.

If you are visiting the Website from the United Kingdom, European Union or European Economic Area, you may review your rights and our privacy practices here.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE TO BOUND TREE

Personal information we collect from you may include:

  • Contact Information: such as your name, email address, postal address, and phone number.
  • Account and Login Information: such as your username, password, security questions, and basic details about your account. Some forms on the Website may require you to provide certain information in order to submit the form. You may elect to not provide this information but doing so may prevent you from using or accessing those features.
  • Payment Information: In order to access some of our services or purchase our products, you may also be required to provide us with payment information, including credit card number, billing address, shipping address, and other information necessary to complete a purchase. Bound Tree takes great care to protect this information and to comply with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI DSS). Bound Tree also does not store payment card information after it has been processed for order fulfillment purposes.
  • Mailing List Data: such as marketing and communications preferences and interests.
  • Purchase Data: such as information regarding your purchase history with us.
  • Audio, electronic, visual or similar information, including photographs and CCTV footage.
  • Professional Information: such as employment details, education information, occupational history or business relationships.
  • Other information you may choose to provide directly to us.

We collect the aforementioned personal information from you so that we can serve you better, and so that we can provide you with our products and services. For example, we may ask you to provide certain personal information when you wish to purchase a product, when you request a repair service, or when you subscribe to one of our newsletters. Should you choose to enroll in our voluntary rewards program, we will use your basic contact information to track your purchases and accumulate points to your account. We also use the information you provide us to initiate returns, issue product safety notices, and communicate with you.

INFORMATION AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED

Bound Tree may also automatically collect certain information about you when you access or use the Website (Usage Information). Usage Information may include your device identifier, IP address, browser type, location (country only), operating system, and other information regarding your use of the Website

We may obtain this Usage Information through the following means:

  • Log information. Log information is data about your use of the Website, such as IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, operating system, date/time stamps, and related data. Log information may be stored in log files.
  • Information collected by cookies and other tracking technologies. Cookies, web beacons or "tracking pixels," embedded scripts, location-identifying technologies (country only), device recognition solutions, and other tracking technologies ("Tracking Technologies") may be used to collect information about your interactions with the Website. Brief descriptions of these Tracking Technologies are provided below.
  • Cookies.Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your computer, smart phone, or tablet when you access the Website. This information can be read by the Website when you return to them. Bound Tree uses cookies to speed up your future visits by storing preferences and login information you provide, and to support a better experience through tailored content. In addition, cookies provide statistical and other information to Bound Tree about how the Website is used in order for Bound Tree to monitor access and use of the sites, assess the effectiveness of particular content, and suggest areas for improvement. Information collected by cookies may be also be used to streamline communications with you by facilitating targeted content, such as suggested products.
    • Cookies may remain on your hard drive for an extended period of time. The Website may associate some or all of these cookies with your device(s). If you use your browsers method of blocking or removing cookies, some but not all types of cookies may be deleted and/or blocked and as a result, some features of the Website may not be available. For more information about cookies, visit: http://www.aboutcookies.org/. Click here to review our Cookie Policy.
  • Web Beacons ("Tracking Pixels"). Web beacons are small graphic images, also known as "internet tags" or "clear gifs," embedded in web pages. Web beacons may be used, without limitation, to count the number of visitors to the Website, to monitor how users navigate the Website, and to count content views.
  • Embedded Scripts. An embedded script is programming code designed to collect information about your interactions with the Website. It is temporarily downloaded onto your computer from our web server, or from a third party with which we work, and is active only while you are connected to the Website. It is deleted or deactivated once you are no longer connected.
  • Device Recognition Technologies. Technologies, including application of statistical probability to data sets, as well as linking a common unique identifier to different device use (e.g., Facebook ID), which attempt to recognize or make assumptions about users and devices (e.g., that a user of multiple devices is the same user or household).

Location-Identifying Technologies
GPS (global positioning systems) software, geo-filtering and other location-aware technologies locate (sometimes precisely) you for purposes such as verifying your location and delivering or restricting content based on your location. Our content may be personalized based on various information we may have about you to try to provide you with more location-relevant content.

Some information regarding your use of the Website and certain third-party services may be collected by Tracking Technologies across time and across our different services. Bound Tree and third parties may use this information to assist you with account log-ins, to track and accumulate your loyalty points, and to offer you products we think may interest you.

Apart from collecting Usage Information, we do not automatically collect information from users who are visiting public areas of the Website and who do not affirmatively provide us with more information. This Usage Information is used exclusively for internal purposes, such as analysis of clickstream data and website optimization.
