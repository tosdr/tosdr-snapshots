School Reading List Policies

At the School Reading List, we are committed to providing a safe, enjoyable, and informative online environment for our visitors and users. To ensure transparency, clarify expectations, and protect your rights and privacy, we have established a set of policies that govern our website’s operations.

These policies encompass a wide range of areas, from acceptable use and ethical guidelines to how we handle your personal information and content on our platform. We encourage you to take a few moments to familiarise yourself with our policies to understand how we operate, what we expect from our users, and how we safeguard your interests.

Our policies are designed to:

  • Protect your rights and privacy.
  • Promote responsible and ethical use of our website.
  • Outline our commitment to diversity and inclusion.
  • Clarify our stance on copyright, images, and content ownership.
  • Provide guidance on providing actionable feedback.
  • Explain our affiliations and funding sources.
  • Share our commitment to ethical principles.
  • Detail our publishing principles and correction procedures.

Your use of our website is subject to these policies, so it’s important to understand them. If you have any questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We value your trust and strive to maintain a safe and enjoyable digital space for all our users.

Thank you for being a part of the School Reading List community, and we hope you find our policies to be informative and reassuring.
