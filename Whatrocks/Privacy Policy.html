As the powerhouse of the WhatRocks service as defined below, WhatRocks Inc. (“WhatRocks”, “we”, or “us”) operates on behalf and in the name of WhatRocks Foundation, the non-profit organization that owns and governs the WhatRocks service, to build a global community of registered engaged, purpose-driven individuals, corporations (including but not limited to advertisers, publishers, and event organizers), and charities (each a “Member”) that take action together to fund social and environmental change:

1 - Corporations reward Individuals financially with fungible blockchain tokens issued exclusively by WhatRocks and called Rocks™ (“Digital Tokens”) when Individuals are exposed to, engage or interact with WhatRocks-enhanced marketing or advertising material such as online banner ads, video or rich media ads, emails, websites and mobile apps (including notifications) as well as any other form of digital or print material.

2 - Individuals use their Digital Tokens to make direct donations to the Charities of their choice or to make giveback purchases of goods and services such as tickets and promo codes to local events and parties (“Giveback Items”) from selling Members (“Sellers”) who donate the Digital Tokens they have received as payment to the Charities of their own choice.

The mission of WhatRocks is to bring to life the vision of WhatRocks Foundation that individuals everywhere be financially empowered by the marketing and advertising spend of advertisers, so they get money to make donations to the social and environmental causes they care about. In addition, we use pixel tags (also referred to as clear GIFs, web beacons, or web bugs).
