This was Vlare, a place to Ignite Your Creativity.

The Vlare Project was discontinued on July 25th, 2021.

We'd like to thank everyone who supported the redevelopment of Vlare.

While, there are no direct plans of restarting development, we are continuing to keep an open mind about potentially having it's return one day in the future, but we cannot make any promises.

We are looking to provide the community with their old videos and channel assets that they might've lost. We do not have a set date for when this will be available nor can we confirm its feasibility, but we will update our Twitter/X when we have more information to share.

- The Vlare Team
