What information we collect and why​

We collect personal information in order to provide and improve our products and services. This information includes:​

  • Information you provide to us: We receive and store any information you provide in relation to the Sites or Games.

  • Automatically-collected information: We automatically collect and store certain types of information about your use of the Sites or Games. This may include information gathered from your device for the purpose of preventing cheating or other unauthorized activity in Games. We also use “cookies” and other unique identifiers, and we obtain certain types of information when your web browser or device accesses Sites or Games.

  • Information from other sources: We might receive information about you from other sources, such as information relating to authentication or purchase information from platforms that interact with the Games or Sites.

If you publicly disclose information about yourself on any Site or Game, such content may not be subject to the privacy protections set forth herein.​

How we use or share your information

We may use the information we collect to help us provide, improve, and promote the Games and Sites. These purposes include:​

  • Providing and improving the Games and Sites;

  • Facilitating purchases;

  • Communicating with you (for example, via email);

  • Conducting data analytics;

  • Complying with our legal obligations; and

  • Engaging in interest-based advertising;

  • Security, moderation, anti-cheat, and anti-fraud activities.

​We may share some of the information we collect to help operate, improve, and support the Games and Sites. Examples include sharing:​

  • with console or platform partners and game developers;

  • with your consent.

​We may also share information that does not identify you with third parties, including aggregate or de-identified information.​

Our use of cookies and tracking

The Sites use cookies for gathering anonymous information such as browser type and operating system, tracking the number of visitors to the site, and understanding how visitors use the site.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Sites due to dependencies of functionality. 

Our use of third party analytics provider and interest-based advertising

We may host advertising on some Sites or Games, as well as embed advertising technologies from third parties. In addition to the tracking technologies we place, other companies may set their own cookies or similar tools when you visit the Sites or use the Games. This includes companies we’ve engaged to provide services on our behalf (like website analytics), as well as third parties that deliver content or offers (like ads from ad networks) contained on the Sites. We may receive reports from these companies on an individual or aggregate basis.

For information accessed via YouTube API Services, you can revoke any Site’s access to this information via the Google security settings page when signed into your relevant Google account.

However, please note that no server, communications network, or data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. The Sites are no exception. We cannot guarantee the security of any information transmitted through the Sites and make no assurances about our ability to prevent any such loss or misuse.

It’s up to you whether you’d like to share private information with us; if you do, you’re acknowledging and agreeing that you’re doing so at your own risk.

Information is kept only as long as necessary for the purposes set out above (or as otherwise communicated to you); after that point, data may be kept only for statistical purposes.
