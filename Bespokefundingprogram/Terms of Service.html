Dashboard Features

Account Metrics

Access in-depth analysis on your funded accounts and challenges highlighting your trade history, account statistics and many metrics that you can utilise to help aid in your improvement as a Funded Trader with Bespoke Funding Program.

Request Payouts

Request a payout seamlessly through the “Payouts” section of the BFP Dashboard. As a Funded BFP Trader, we always encourage to “pay the trader” and thus when your account is eligible, request a payout here.

Access Certificates

Access all of your BFP Funded Trader Certificates in the “Certificates” portal, download and share them to prove your skill as a funded trader. Once you have passed your account challenge, your certificates will display here.
