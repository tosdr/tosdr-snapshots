Protecting Your privacy

Virgin Australia is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This commitment is demonstrated in our Privacy Policies which tell you how we manage your personal information and how to contact us if you have any privacy concerns. 

If our Privacy Policies do not answer a question that you have about how we manage your personal information, please call or email us.

  • Read the Virgin Australia Privacy Policy 
  • Read the Velocity Frequent Flyer Privacy Policy

For specific information on the types of information that we collect about you and why we do this, you should read the appropriate privacy statement:

  • Privacy Statement for Virgin Australia (Bookings)
  • Privacy Statement for Velocity (Membership Join)
  • Privacy Statement for Virgin Australia Business Flyer and Loyalty
  • Privacy Statement for Virgin Australia V-Mail
  • Privacy Statement for Virgin Australia Lounge
  • Privacy Statement for CCTV Images
  • Privacy Statement for Health Information

Contact us

If you have a concern about your privacy or wish to make a privacy complaint, you can contact us by:

  1. Calling us
  2. Writing to us at the below address:
