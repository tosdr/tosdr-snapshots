Section 2. Information We Collect

Weee! collects personal information when you interact with our Services. Personal information is any information that identifies or makes an individual identifiable. The definition of personal information (used interchangeably in this Privacy Policy with the term "personal data") depends on the applicable law based on your physical location. Only the definition that applies to your physical location will apply to you under this Privacy Policy. The types of personal information that we may collect about you include, but are not limited to: information you provide to us, information collected automatically about your use of our Services, and information from third parties.

This includes:

  • Account Registration. We collect basic account information such as your email address, phone number, username and password. We need this information to create your account and it helps secure and provide access to our Services. We also obtain or infer demographic information, as well as profile photos if you choose to upload one. If you use Facebook or another service such as Google or WeChat to register for or log in to the Services (or later connect your accounts), you authorize Facebook or the other service, as the case may be, to share your personal information with us, including your email address, age and publicly available information about you and your Facebook friends, if you use Facebook for registering for the Services. Please note that when you access our Services through social media channels or when you connect our Services to social media services, you are authorizing us to collect, store, and use such information and content in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

     

  • Form Submissions and Newsletter Signups. We collect information that you provide by filling in forms that we make available. This includes when making purchases from our Services or requesting information or assistance. We also collect information relating to our newsletter subscription page, including name and email address. In addition, we collect the email address of others if you refer a friend as part of our Weeebates referral. Please have permission in order to share their information with us.

     

  • Program Participation and Promotions. We collect information related to your participation in our programs, including our Weee! VIP program, our registered affiliate program, or promotions or surveys.

     

  • Services Usage. We gather information from the actions you take to recommend, create, communicate and share content. This includes messages you send, posts you create yourself, and the products you mark as favorites or share. In addition, we track social interactions in our Apps (e.g. what you bought, what you loved, and your posts and invites).

     

  • Customer Service and Technical Support. If you send us support requests or comments you may also provide us with additional information to enable us to contact you and address your feedback, and we may retain such information to enhance our Services and to respond to you.

     

  • Payment Information. When you purchase products or services from Weee!, you provide our payment processor with your payment information. We currently use third parties to process such payments but may change that in the future.

     

  • Job Applications. We collect employment- and background-related information provided to us as a part of the job application process.

     

  • Device Permissions. We collect information relating to your use of features of our Services that may ask you to grant us access to location information, photos, microphone, video camera, or media on your computer or mobile device.

     

We also collect information automatically as you navigate and interact with our Services. For instance, when you browse our Site, our systems automatically collect information such as your web request, Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser language, domain names, referring and exit pages and URLs, platform type, pages viewed and the order of these page views, the amount of time spent on particular pages, and the date and time of your request.
