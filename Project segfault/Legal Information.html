Legal FAQ

What do I do if a user is disturbing me?

If you are being disturbed by a user on our services, then you should immediately contact us. You can do that by sending us an email or through our Matrix support channel. If you prefer to keep your report private on Matrix, contact one of our team members directly. You can find their info on our team page.
