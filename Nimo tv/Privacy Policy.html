You may provide a variety of information about yourself to us when you register for NIMO Services; upload, purchase, view, or download certain content or products from the NIMO Services; or otherwise use the features and functionality of the NIMO Services. The information you provide to us include:

  • Account information includes, username user ID or similar identifier, date of birth, gender.

  • Device information includes data from which your device could be identified, such as device serial number, or about your device, such as browser type.
  • Transaction information in case of purchases, it may include details about payments to and from you and other details of products and services you have purchased or received from us.

  • Biometric information (only for those who stream via Nimo TV) includes information regarding your facial features. Before collecting your biometric information we will inform you separately and ask for your consent.

We will collect your facial features when you use this function for face recognition and use it strictly within the scope of your authorized consent, and we will confirm with you again if we intend to use your facial information to provide you with other products and/or service features in the future. We will neither use the results of such operations for other purposes nor upload or store them on the server. These information is not shared with any third parties.
