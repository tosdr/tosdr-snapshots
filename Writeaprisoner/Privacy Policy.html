2. What types of information do we collect?

“Information,” as used in this Privacy Policy, means personal information that identifies, relates to, describes, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular user or household.

We collect and/or have collected over the past twelve (12) months the following categories of information about you and other users of the Website:

  • Information You Provide to Us. We collect information from you when you voluntarily provide it to us over the Website, including as part of registering a user account. Such information may include your name, email address, telephone number(s), street address, city, and state. Additionally, if you are an inmate, we may collect your eye color, hair color, hometown, date of birth, the address you plan to reside upon release, your earliest release date, your latest release date, your incarcerated since date, whether you are serving a life sentence or if you are on death row, and your criminal history including the crime you were arrested for, the arresting agency, and the city and state where the crime was committed.
  • Content You Make Public. Company collects information from you that you voluntarily upload to the Website for public display (“Public Information”), such as your Content, which may include posts, comments, photos, text, and artwork. Once you submit Public Information, neither you nor Company are able to control how it may be copied, shared, or used by third parties, even if you and/or Company later delete such Public Information. Accordingly, you should carefully consider what personal information you choose to make public.
  • Transaction and Payment Information. We collect information from you, as needed, to process payments to administer your participation on the Website, such as your bank account number, payment card number, expiration date, card verification number, and billing address. Specifically, we use third-party integration through Authorize.Net to process payments. For more information about Authorize.Net’s privacy practices, visit https://usa.visa.com/legal/privacy-policy.html.
  • Device/Internet Connection Information. When you use the Website, we automatically gather information made available by your web browser (such as Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome), internet service provider (such as Comcast or Cox), and device (such as your computer, phone, tablet, or fitness tracker), depending on your settings for each. For example, we may collect your IP address, information about the operating system or type of device you use, the device ID associated with your device, the date and time you access the Website, and your log in attempts. 
  • Internet Activity Information. As explained below, we may also collect information about your browsing history, search history, and information regarding your interaction with a particular website, application, or advertisement, including the Website. We may collect information regarding the websites, application, or advertisement you visited immediately before and after your use of the Website.
  • Geolocation Information. We may collect information about where you are located, both generally and at the time you access the Website. For example, we may collect your mailing address or ascertain your approximate location from your IP address.
  • Protected Class Information. Due to the specific nature of our services, you may be asked to provide information about your race, ethnicity, nationality, gender, age, marital or family status, sexual orientation, religion, and/or whether you served in the military.
  • Education and Employment Information. We may collect information about your educational background including your highest level of education, previous schools attended, your education goals, whether you are currently in school, and any recent educational accomplishments. We may also collect your employment history and employment goals.
  • Other Information. From time to time, we may collect other information from you, such as when you make a claim for a promotion run on the Website, when you request assistance through our Website, or when you voluntarily subscribe to promotions, reminders, or newsletters. You will always be asked for your consent in advance.
