What We Do

Our Site helps connect inmates who are seeking non-incarcerated pen-pals with whom to correspond.

Since our inception, we have expanded from providing pen-pal services to providing inmates with the opportunity to post legal, employment, and reintegration profiles; organizing book donations; and services aimed directly toward reducing recidivism and raising awareness. Upon release, we also provide inmates with reintegration kits and various self-help documents.

  • We do not do background checks on any user of the Services, including inmates.
