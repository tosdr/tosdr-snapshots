Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Nestlé USA and its affiliates and subsidiaries (referred to as “Nestlé,”the “Company,” “we,” or “our” as applicable) collect, use, and disclose information about you. This Privacy Policy applies to information that we collect when you use or access our websites (each a “Site”), mobile applications (each an “App”), and services (collectively the “Services”) that display or link to this Privacy Policy, or when you otherwise interact with us. 

In some cases, we may provide different or additional policies or notices about our information practices. For instance, depending on where you reside our “Notice at Collection and U.S. State Privacy Notice” may apply in addition to this Privacy Policy. Please refer to that notice for state-specific disclosure requirements and information about exercising certain privacy rights. 

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about our information practices and the choices available to you. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our information practices, please contact us.

This Policy does not cover information collected about you when you interact with us in your capacity as a business-to-business (“B2B”) contact. To learn more about our information practices in B2B situations, please read our B2B Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of data about you, as described below.

Contact and account information. We collect information when you make a purchase or create an account or profile with us, including, your name, address, phone number, email address, username, password, purchase history, and any other information you choose to provide. 

Interests and demographic information. In order to better understand our customers and offer products and services of interest to you, we may collect information from or about you that indicates your interests or demographic information. Examples may include date of birth, age or age range, gender, geographic location (e.g., ZIP Code), favorite products, hobbies and interests, household or lifestyle data, or transactional information about the products or services that you purchase. This may include data that indicates your work status, professional credentials, dietary preferences, nutrition, and exercise habits and goals. It may also include data about your child’s name, age, and gender.

Payment information. If you make a purchase, we work with a third-party payment processor to collect and process your billing address and payment method information.

Customer service and feedback. We may collect information from you when you request customer support or information from us, provide feedback or reviews about your experience with us or our products, or otherwise communicate with or contact us.

Social media account information. If you link a third-party social media account with us, we may collect information you have publicly shared on your account.

Consumer-Generated Content. We may collect content that you create and then share with us on third-party social networks or one of our Sites or Apps. Examples include photos, videos, personal stories, product reviews, or other similar media or content. Where permitted, we collect and publish consumer-generated content in connection with a variety of activities, including contests and other promotions, Site community features, consumer engagement, and third-party social networking.

Location information. We may collect or infer information about your location based on your mailing address, zip code, or IP address.

Device and usage information. We collect information about the device you use to access our Services, including technical information about your device and network, such as hardware model, operating system version, browser, mobile carrier, device identifiers, and IP address. We also collect information about your activity on our Services and your interactions with our ads and communications, such as the routes by which you access our Services along with access dates and times, browsing behavior such as pages viewed, links clicked, information about your activity on a specific page, such as mouse movements and keystrokes, and items placed in your cart or added to your wish list.

How We Collect Information

There are several ways we may obtain information about you. Nestlé collects information from you (a) that you choose to share with us when using our Services and when you otherwise interact with us, such as through our customer service channels, (b) that we collect automatically when you use our Services, and (c) that we receive from third parties.

We collect information from you directly. We collect information when you use our Services or otherwise interact with us. This includes placing an order, providing a review, signing up for marketing communications, registering for an account, entering a promotion or contest, or participating in our surveys.

We collect information automatically. We and our third party service providers collect data about you using cookies,pixels, session replay, and similar technologies.. We use these technologies to improve our Services provide certain functionality, analyze use of our Services and enhance your experience, see which areas and features of our Services are popular, and count visits. They also help us understand information like access times, pages viewed, links clicked, and the page you visited before navigating to our Services. For more information about our use of cookies and other tracking technologies, see the “Targeted Advertising and Analytics” and “Your Choices” sections below.

We get information about you from other sources. We may collect information about you from our business partners or affiliates. We may also acquire information from other sources , such as when you place an online order through a third-party, to help us maintain the accuracy of the information we collect, personalize your experience with the Services, target our communications so that we can inform you of products and services or other offers that may be of interest to you, measure ad quality and responses, and for internal business analysis or other business purposes. We may also receive information from our “tell-a-friend” program, our “text me the recipe” button, or friends who want to send you a product.

Use of Information

We may use the information that we collect from and about you for a variety of business purposes, as follows:

  • We use information to communicate with you. We may use information to answer your questions and comments, including providing you with information about this Privacy Policy or our Terms and Conditions. We may also use information to provide you with customer service.
  • We use information so you can purchase our products. We may use information to process and fulfill orders. We may use information to process your registration with our Services so you can use our features.
  • We use information to improve our products and Services. We may use information to make our Services better.
  • We use information to personalize the Services. We may use information to personalize your experience with us, including saving preferences or settings. This may include customizing the content you see or making it easier for you to log into your account. In order to personalize the Services, we may analyze your preferences, information, and habits.
  • We use information for marketing purposes. We provide you with information about new products and special offers or promotions which may include advertisements for our products and services that are tailored to you. We may use information to run a sweepstakes or contest. We deliver content to you through various channels, such as email and social media platforms. If you wish to opt out of marketing communications, please see the “Your Choices” section below.
  • We use information to protect our company and others. We use information to  detect, investigate, respond to, prosecute, and  help to protect against security incidents and other malicious, deceptive, fraudulent, or illegal activity, and help protect the rights and property of Nestlé and others. 
  • We use information for other lawful purposes or as we may disclose to you.

Targeted Advertising and Analytics

We engage others to provide analytics, serve advertisements, and perform related services across the web and in mobile apps. These entities may use cookies, web beacons, software development kits (SDKs), device identifiers, and other technologies to collect information about your use of our Services and other websites and mobile apps, including your IP address, web browser, mobile network information, pages viewed, time spent on pages or in mobile apps, links clicked, and conversion information. This information is used to deliver advertising targeted to your interests on other companies’ sites or mobile apps and to analyze and track data, determine the popularity of certain content and better understand your activity.

You can opt-out of having your personal information used or disclosed for these purposes via cookies and similar technologies by clicking on the “Your Privacy Choices  ” link located on the footer of our websites and following the instruction in the pop-up window.  Your opt-out choice will be linked to your browser only; therefore, you will need to renew your op-out choice if you visit our websites from a new device, browser, or if you clear your browser’s cookies. From within our apps, you can opt out by navigating to the Privacy Information section under your account.

In addition to cookie-based ad targeting, we may disclose identifiers, such as your email address and phone number, to our advertising partners. These advertising partners translate that information into a unique identifier that they can then use to show ads that are more relevant to you across the web and in mobile apps. You may opt out of these disclosures by filling out our webform.

You can also learn more about interest-based ads, or opt-out of having your web browsing information used for behavioral advertising purposes by companies that participate in the Digital Advertising Alliance, by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices. Please note that you will need to renew your opt-out choice if you visit our Services from a new device or browser, or if you clear your cookies. Your device may also include a feature that allows you to opt out of having certain information collected through mobile apps used for targeted advertising purposes.

Disclosure of Information

We disclose information  described in the Targeted Advertising and Analytics section and in the following ways: 

We disclose information within our family of companies. We may disclose information with the Nestlé S.A. family of companies, which includes all direct and indirect subsidiaries of parent company Nestlé S.A. and any future related companies.

We disclose information to our vendors and service providers. We make personal information available to our vendors, service providers, contractors and consultants who perform services on our behalf, such as companies that assist us with web hosting, shipping and delivery, payment processing, financing, fraud prevention, customer service, data enrichment, analytics, and marketing.

We disclose information to any successor to all or part of our business. In the event that Nestlé USA or our assets are acquired by or merged with another company, we may disclose all categories of information as part of that transaction. This acquisition or merger may include part of our business or one of our brands. This may occur in the event of bankruptcy.

We disclose information if we think we have to in order to comply with the law or to protect ourselves. For example, we may disclose all categories of information to respond to a court order or subpoena. We will disclose information if a government agency or regulatory body requests it. This includes U.S. and non-U.S. law enforcement or regulatory authorities.

We disclose aggregate or anonymous information. We may disclose aggregate, anonymous, or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you. We maintain and use this information only in a de-identified fashion and will not attempt to re-identify such information, except as permitted by law.

We disclose information as permitted by law and for other reasons we may describe to you.

Your Choices

Account Information. You can access, correct, or delete certain information stored within your online account by logging into your account at any time.

Cookies and Similar Tracking Technologies.  We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze visits to and use of our Services and how consumers are interacting with our marketing communications. You can usually adjust your browser settings to remove or reject browser cookies. You can also adjust certain cookie settings by clicking on the “Your Privacy Choices” link found in the footer of our websites and following the instructions in the pop-up window, or by navigating to the Privacy Information section under your account within the Nestlé apps. Please note that removing or rejecting cookies could affect the functionality of our Services, such as remembering your preferred language. 

Communication preferences. You may opt-out of receiving text messages, web push notifications or promotional emails from us at any time. To opt-out of text messages, please send a reply text that says “STOP”. To opt-out of web push notifications, please update your notification preferences in your browser settings. To opt-out of promotional emails, please follow the unsubscribe instructions in those communications. If you opt-out, we may still send you non-promotional emails, such as those about your account,  or orders.

These Services and Apps are Not Intended For Children

The Services where this Privacy Policy appears are meant for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or legal guardian and think your child has given us information, you can write to us or email us at the addresses provided at the end of this Privacy Policy. Please mark your inquiries “Parental Information Request.” Parents, you can learn more about how to protect children’s privacy here.

Security Measures and Location of Information

We use standard security measures. While we make every effort to help ensure the integrity and security of our network and systems, the Internet is not 100% secure. We cannot promise that your use of our Services will be completely safe. We encourage you to use caution, and not to share or reuse passwords.

Transfer of Information to the U.S. and Other Countries. Nestlé USA is based in the United States. You understand that by interacting with us your information will be sent to the US. The laws of the US may not provide the same protections as the laws in your country.

Additional Disclosures for Specific Jurisdictions

Some U.S. states have privacy laws that require specific disclosures and afford residents of those states with additional privacy rights.
