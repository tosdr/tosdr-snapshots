Refund policy

This policy is for all returns, exchanges or refunds pertaining to the Cleetus McFarland Merchandise and all affiliated parties.


How to submit a return or exchange request

  • Read the below return and exchange policies
  • Log into your account
  • Click order you want to submit the return for
  • If your order has more than one item, select the items you would like to return
  • Click "Request Return"

We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance as we can not guarantee we will receive your returned item.

Any refund will automatically be applied to the original credit card or method of payment. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.

Returns:

  • 30-day return window
  • Customeris responsible for return shipping costs
  • Approved returns are subject to 10% restocking fee + our shipping cost
  • Sale items, promotional items, clearance items are not eligible.
  • Certain items, as indicated on the item listing, are not returnable
  • No refunds will be provided for orders purchased during a giveaways once a winner has been announced.

  • Items must be unwashed and unworn.We reserve the right to refuse a refund if the items have any sign of wear, alteration, misuse, or damage. This includes items appearing worn, with makeup or deodorant stains, smelling of fragrances or odors, or with animal hair.

Exchange

  • If you would like a different size than ordered, return shipping is the responsibility of the customer and subject to availability.
  • Customeris responsible for returnshipping costs
  • Item(s) may be exchanged on an order once.
  • No international exchanges
  • Any price differences between sizes will be invoiced to the customer
  • Open a refund request as outlined above, specify the item you would like in exchange


Order Returned to Sender (RTS)

  • For items returned by the mail carrier:If an incorrect address was entered by the customer, emailsupport@cleetusmcfarland.comimmediately. If a package is sent with an incorrect address entered by the customer, we will email you, per our discretion we may issue a refund minus10%restocking fee or the customer may pay the shipping fees to resend the package.
  • If the customer does not respond to our method of contact within5 business days, a refund will be issued with a10%deduction for a restocking fee.
