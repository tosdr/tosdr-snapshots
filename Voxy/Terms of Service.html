      15           Group Classes Code of Conduct

      15.1        As a learner participating in Voxy’s group classes, in addition to the other provisions of these Terms, you agree to be respectful of the teacher and your fellow learners by following this code of conduct:

      15.1.1    You arrive to the class on time and stay for the entirety of the class.

      15.1.2    You mute yourself when you’re not speaking so background noise doesn’t interrupt the class.

      15.1.3    If another learner or the teacher is speaking, you allow him/her to finish before speaking.

      15.1.4    You allow others to have equal opportunity to speak in the class.

      15.1.5    You do not use language that makes others uncomfortable, i.e. profane, sexually suggestive, rude or violent language.

      15.1.6    You do not make disrespectful comments about race, religions or countries.

      15.1.7    You wear appropriate attire in the class, i.e. a shirt, pants or other appropriate clothing.

      15.1.8    Your cell phone is turned off or set to vibrate so you and your fellow participants are not distracted during the class.

      15.1.9    The classroom is a place to learn and practice English, so please only use English during classes for both speaking and writing.
