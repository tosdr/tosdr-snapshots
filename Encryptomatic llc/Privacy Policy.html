This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from //www.encryptomatic.com or other sites owned and operated by us (the Site).

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you visit the Site, certain information about your device is collected, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, as you browse the Site, we collect information about the individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site. We refer to this automatically-collected information as â€œDevice Information.

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • Cookies are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. For more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit //www.allaboutcookies.org.

Additionally when you make a purchase or attempt to make a purchase through the Site, we collect certain information from you, including your name, billing address, shipping address, payment information (including credit card numbers, email address, and phone number. When we talk about Personal Information in this Privacy Policy, we are talking both about Device Information and Order Information.

 

Cookies

You can choose to restrict or block access to cookies set by Encryptomatic LLC or any other company. Cookies are small files created by websites you visit. They can make your online experience easier by saving browsing information. With cookies, websites can keep you signed in, remember your site preferences, and give you locally relevant content. You can set your browser to notify you when a web server attempts to write or load a cookie to your computer. This gives you a chance to accept or reject the cookie. Nearly every website on the internet stores cookies when you visit it. For more information about controlling cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

DO NOT TRACK

Although we would like to respect Do Not Track (DNT) requests, we find that we are hamstrung by the larger advertising platforms we need to use to conduct our business, and therefor we have little control over web browser Do Not Track requests. Companies like Google, for example, do not natively respect DNT and limit our ability to force then to respect DNT. In the past we've tried writing JS code to only fire Google Analytics requests if DNT is not detected, but this simply has not been effective or stable. Since using Google's platform is a necessary way for us to reach customers with news about our products, it places us in a difficult "all or nothing situation, where we either respect DNT by removing the entire Google platform, or we stay in business by using those platforms without any resonable control over DNT. You may be able to use Google's analytic's opt-out browser add-in to impact DNT requests. It's a worth a look, anyway. For the time being, until we ourselves are able to effectively respect DNT requests, or until major platform provides like Google provide us this capability, please assume that we unable to, and therefor do not, alter our Site's data collection and use practices when your browser sends a Do Not Track signal.

Additionally, if you are a European resident we note that we are processing your information in order to fulfill contracts we might have with you (for example if you make an order through the Site), or otherwise to pursue our legitimate business interests listed above. Additionally, please note that your information will be transferred outside of Europe to our servers in the United States.

 

DATA RETENTION

We retain customer contact information indefinitely or at our discretion, so we can provide customer support services, product updates and security notices.
