1. Introduction

Revenue Well Systems (“RevenueWell” or “We”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this privacy notice (“Privacy Notice”).
This Privacy Notice describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide as a subscriber of our service when you visit the websites www.revenuewell.com and www.patientconnect365.com (each, a “Website”) and our mobile applications that you download from an official mobile application store, such as the such as the Apple App Store® or the Google Play Store® which may provide dedicated non-browser based interaction between you and our Websites (each, an “Application”), and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

This Privacy Notice applies to information we collect:

  • On our Websites and Applications.
  • In email, text, and other electronic messages between you, as a subscriber of RevenueWell, and our Websites and/or Application.

It does not apply to information collected by:

  • Us offline or through any other means, including on any other website operated any third party; or
  • Any third party, including through any application or content (including advertising) that may link to or be accessible from or on the Websites or the Application and including any information collected by your healthcare provider. 

This Privacy Notice does not apply to information we receive about you as a patient of one of our subscribers and who may use our products and services as a result of their relationship with their healthcare provider. This may include information we receive about you from your healthcare provider as well as information we receive when you fill out a form on our Websites provided by your healthcare provider for an office visit or otherwise. This information is subject to your healthcare provider’s Notice of Privacy Practices and we only process such information in accordance with our agreement with your healthcare provider. Your healthcare provider, and not RevenueWell, is responsible for obtaining any required consents from you for the use of your personal information, including obtaining any required consents to communicate with you via SMS or text messages. If we contact you on behalf of your healthcare provider, you may opt-out from receiving such communications by clicking “update preferences” or “unsubscribe” in our emails, emailing us at [email protected] or, if you have received a text message, by sending “STOP” in response to such text message. We are not responsible for any personal health information you may transmit through our products and services. If you need to access or correct any information we have received about you from your healthcare provider, please contact your healthcare provider to access and update such information as permitted by law. 

Please read this Privacy Notice carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Websites or the Application. By accessing or using our Websites or the Application, you agree to this Privacy Notice. This Privacy Notice may change from time to time (see Changes to Our Privacy Notice). Your continued use of our Websites or Application after we make changes is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the Privacy Notice periodically for updates. 

2. Children Under the Age of 18

Our Websites and Application is not intended for children under 18 years of age. No one under age 18 may provide any information to or on the Websites or Application. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are under 18, do not use or provide any information on our Websites or the Application or through any of their respective features, register on the Websites or Application, or otherwise provide any information about yourself to us, including your name, address, telephone number, email address, or any screen name or user name you may use. If we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child under 18 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information. If you believe we might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us through the contact information below.

Generally

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Websites and Application, including information:

  • About your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our Websites or Application, and usage details.

We collect this information:

  • Directly from you when you provide it to us.
  • Automatically as you navigate through the site. Information collected automatically may include usage details, IP addresses, device information, and information collected through cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies.
  • From third parties, for example, our business partners.

For Physicians and Dentists

We also collect information regarding your patients. Our collection and use of this information is not governed by this Privacy Notice and instead is governed by the applicable agreements with you for our services, including any applicable Business Associate Agreement.

Information You Provide to Us

The information we collect on or through our Websites or Application may include:

  • Information that you provide by filling in forms on our Websites or Application or as part of using or configuring our services. This includes information provided at the time of subscribing to our services, managing your subscription to our services, and requesting further information or a demonstration. We may also ask you for information when you report a problem with our Website, Application, or our services.
  • Records and copies of your correspondence (including email addresses), if you contact us.
  • Your responses to surveys that we might ask you to complete for research purposes.
  • Details of transactions (including communications with patients) you carry out through our Websites or Application.

Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies

As you navigate through and interact with our Websites or Application, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns, including:

  • Details of your visits to our Websites or Application, including traffic data, geolocation data, logs, referring/exit pages, date and time of your visit to our Websites or Application, error information, clickstream data, and other communication data and the resources that you access and use on the Websites or Application.
  • Information about your computer or mobile device and internet connection, including your IP address, make and model of your mobile device, mobile device phone number, unique device identifier, advertising identifier, operating system, and browser type.

The information we collect automatically may include personal information, or we may maintain it or associate it with personal information we collect in other ways or receive from third parties. It helps us to improve our Websites and Application and to deliver a better and more personalized service, including by enabling us to:

  • Estimate our audience size and usage patterns.
  • Store information about your preferences, allowing us to customize our Websites or Application according to your individual interests.
  • Recognize you when you return to our Websites or Application.

The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:

  • Cookies (or browser cookies). A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of our Websites or Application. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you direct your browser to our Websites or use our Application.
