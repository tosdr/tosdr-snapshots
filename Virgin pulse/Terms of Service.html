Last Updated: December 1, 2023

The Program is paid for by your employer, your spouses employer or another organization interested in your health and wellbeing (the Program Sponsor) and operated by Virgin Pulse, Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Delaware, headquartered at 75 Fountain Street, Providence, Rhode Island, 02902 (Virgin Pulse). This Membership Agreement (the Agreement) addresses the terms for becoming and being a Member of our Program and initiating your Membership. Please carefully read through this Agreement. To join the Program and enjoy the Virgin Pulse services and Program benefits you must create an account. By creating an account and checking the I Agree box you agree to all the provisions contained in this Agreement. Accessing our websites is also subject to additional terms contained in the Virgin Pulse website Terms of Use located at https://www.virginpulse.com/terms-of-use/.

Virgin Pulse offers a number of wellness programs under different program names including Engage, Ignite, Core, and VP Go. This Agreement applies to all Virgin Pulse wellness programs unless there is an alternate Membership Agreement published specifically for that program.

Your Program Sponsor may elect to give you access to the Virgin Pulse Transform Program, as well.  In this Agreement, we provide a Transform Program summary that describes terms that differ from the Wellness Program rights and benefits.

What are Health Stations?

Your Sponsor has the option to install Health Stations. Health Stations include a mobile device, such as a laptop or tablet, paired with a scale and blood pressure cuff. The Health Stations provide limited functionality. Members may use these stations to collect weight and blood pressure information for the Program. To use the Health Station, you must log in using your account information. You are responsible for logging out of the Health Station to protect your account. We do not guarantee the measurement devices are accurate. If you are concerned about your weight and blood pressure, please contact your health care provider.

What are Coaching Services?

The Program may include Coaching Services. Coaching services includes interaction with a live coach who provides guidance on wellness topics, such as diet, exercise, and sleep. The terms of this Membership Agreement apply to all Coaching Services.

We may terminate your account according to the terms of the honesty, accuracy and conduct terms explained above.

If your account is cancelled, we will notify you that your Membership and this Agreement will terminate 62 days after the cancellation process has started. Our notification will include instructions on how you can redeem any outstanding incentive rewards, including PulseCash. After the cancellation process is completed, you will:

  • no longer be able to participate in the Program or access any of the Program services;
  • no longer be entitled to receive any benefits or to earn any rewards, including PulseCash;
  • not be able to redeem any unused rewards, including PulseCash, and
  • forfeit any incentive rewards, including PulseCash, you may have earned but have not redeemed as of the cancellation date.

Text messaging may not be available via all carriers. You are responsible for providing us an accurate mobile phone number for communications, and for updating us if that mobile phone number changes. You are responsible for the security of your mobile phone, including activating passwords and enabling encryption to protect messages stored on the phone.

We do not guarantee the successful delivery of text messages by your wireless provider. Messages sent by text may not be delivered if the mobile device is not in range of a transmission site, or if sufficient network capacity is not available at a particular time. Even within a coverage area, factors beyond the control of wireless carriers may interfere with message delivery, including the terrain, proximity to buildings, foliage, weather, and the recipients equipment. We will not be liable for losses or damages arising from (a) non-delivery, delayed delivery, or misdirected delivery of a text message; (b) inaccurate or incomplete content in a text message; or (c) use or reliance on the content of any text message for any purpose.

Because we cannot encrypt text messages, there is a risk that this sensitive information could be intercepted or viewed by third parties, including others who have access to your device. When you choose to receive text messages from us, you do so at your own risk.

What precautions should I take to participate in the Virgin Pulse Program?

ALWAYS CONSULT WITH YOUR PHYSICIAN BEFORE STARTING ANY EXERCISE PROGRAM. By enrolling and participating in the Program you certify that you are healthy and that your physical condition allows you to perform moderate to intense exercise. If you experience any physical symptoms such as abnormal or sudden blood pressure changes, fainting, dizziness, or irregular heartbeat or any other physical symptoms which seem abnormal to you while participating in the Program, stop exercising immediately and consult your doctor without delay.
