Privacy policy

Your Privacy & Data Protection

Sonictek Limited is committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect information about our customers for the purpose of processing orders and for sending solicited email special offers. To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

When you visit our site

When you visit the Sonictek Limited web site, you are anonymous. We do not collect any personal information, including your email address, without your prior knowledge and consent. Your Sonictek Limited shopping cart is maintained during your browsing session through the use of a randomly generated number that is stored in a cookie on your computer. None of your personal information is stored in the cookie itself. The cookie is only generated once you place an item into your shopping cart, and it self-destructs when you quit your web browser.

When you place an order with us

To process and ship your order, we need your name, billing address, shipping address, phone number, and email address. This information is transferred to our server using SSL encryption (Secure Sockets Layer), a security protocol that provides communications privacy over the Internet. At no time do we send you unsolicited mail or email, nor will we phone you about any matter not related to your order. We never share our customer information with any outside source.

When you e-mail us

Should you contact us via email or otherwise specify that you want us to correspond with you via email, we will. We will not use casual email contact for anything other than response to your questions. We will not send you unsolicited email or other communications based upon your email contact to Sonictek Limited.

When you leave our site

Sonictek Limited has no control over the privacy practices of sites outside our domain. If you follow a link to another Web site, read the site's privacy policy and contact their administrator with your privacy-related questions.

A special word to parents of children under the age of 13

Sonictek Limited strongly encourages parents of young Web users to play an active role in their children's online activity. Children under the age of 13 should not submit orders, application forms, service requests or other documents to Sonictek Limited without the prior consent of their parent or legal guardian.

Telephone Numbers

We include customer telephone numbers on the courier delivery manifest for contact reasons should they need to get in touch with you about the delivery. Should you not wish your telephone number to be passed on to the courier please indicate this on the order form (please be aware that by not including your telephone number will prevent our courier from contacting you if there is a problem and delivery may be delayed).
If you have questions concerning our Privacy Policy, please email admin@sonictek.co.uk
