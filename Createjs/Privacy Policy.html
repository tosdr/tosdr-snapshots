CreateJS Privacy Policy

The CreateJS libraries are maintained by gskinner (gskinner.com, inc.)

The CreateJS libraries do not log or collect any personal information.

The CreateJS CDN (Content Delivery Network) located at code.createjs.com is hosted by Akamai (Akamai Technologies, Inc.) on an account controlled by Adobe (Adobe Systems Incorporated). Specific information on the personal data collected by these companies is available from their respective web sites.

The use of the CreateJS CDN is provided as a convenience, and is not required to use the CreateJS libraries.
