Privacy Policy

Last Updated October 11, 2022

Tribe of Noise BV ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Free Music Archive - https://www.freemusicarchive.org - website (the "Service").
This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Definitions

Service
Service is the Free Music Archive - https://www.freemusicarchive.org - website operated by Tribe of Noise BV

Personal Data
Personal Data means data about a living individual who can be identified from those data (or from those and other information either in our possession or likely to come into our possession).

Usage Data
Usage Data is data collected automatically either generated by the use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself (for example, the duration of a page visit).

User Generated Content (UGC)
Data that you make public on your Free Music Archive page or the pages of other members.

Cookies
Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer or mobile device).

Data Controller
Data Controller means the natural or legal person who (either alone or jointly or in common with other persons) determines the purposes for which and the manner in which any personal information are, or are to be, processed.
For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, we are a Data Controller of your Personal Data.

Data Processors (or Service Providers)
Data Processor (or Service Provider) means any natural or legal person who processes the data on behalf of the Data Controller.
We may use the services of various Service Providers in order to process your data more effectively.

Data Subject (or User)
Data Subject is any living individual who is using our Service and is the subject of Personal Data.

2. Information Collection And Use

a. Even if you do not log into the Free Music Archive, some information about your visit will be automatically recorded in our server access log. Whenever you browse or access the Site, we will record personal information including, but not limited to, your IP address, browser type, web page(s), video and/or audio file(s) accessed, and the date and time of your visit.

b. When you access the Site, FMA collects some information about your visit using tools like Google Analytics, which collects information such as the country or city where you’re visiting from, tracks you click on to stream or download, how long you stayed on each page, which web browser you used, and similar information. We collect this information to better meet the needs of our visitors, maintain good security practices, monitor for abuse, and to report on grant-funded initiatives that sustain the work of the Site. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using their browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

c. Users who browse the Site without logging in are still recorded as traffic, but visits are not associated with personal information that is used to identify Members of the site such as username, email address, etc.

d. Users who browse the archive through our apps are subject to the policies of iTunes and Google Play, respectively, depending on what operating system they access the app from and what they do within the app.

e. We use aggregated browsing information to observe traffic on the Site and to improve the services and functionality of the Site.

3. Types of Data Collected

Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address
  • Current city
  • Cookies and Usage Data

We may use your Personal Data to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information that may be of interest to you. You may opt out of receiving any, or all, of these communications from us by following the unsubscribe link or instructions provided in any email we send.

Usage Data
We may also collect information how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). User Generated Content
We store information that you make public on your profile page, song pages or pages of other FMA members (e.g. you and other members can give feedback on profiles and songs uploaded). When logged in you can message a FMA member via the “Contact” option. We may keep a copy of your message for legal purposes (e.g. if a FMA member sends spam we can gather proof and block or delete their account).

Advertisements may display on this website and, if so, may set and access cookies on your computer; such cookies are subject to the privacy policy of the parties providing the advertisement. However, the parties providing the advertising do not have access to this site's cookies. These parties usually use non-personally-identifiable or anonymous codes to obtain information about your visits to this site.

For more information about this type of online advertising, about cookies, and about how to turn this feature off, please visit this link.

Please note that turning off advertising cookies won’t mean that you are not served any advertising, merely that it will not be tailored to your interests.
