SECTION 25 – MALWARE DETECTION CAPABILITIES

Certo’s applications are able to detect infections by analyzing the device for known malware signatures. Please note however that they cannot prevent malware infections from occurring.

While every effort is taken to ensure Certo’s products are as accurate as possible, a completed scan with no detections does not guarantee that your device is free from malware. As with any anti-malware tool, we cannot guarantee to detect 100% of malware all of the time, due to new threats emerging constantly. However, we are continually monitoring for new threats and add them to our database as soon as possible.

The malware detection engines built into our apps are very sensitive to allow us to detect a wide range of threats as well as remnants from attacks that have happened in the past. This means that on rare occasions false positives can occur. At Certo, we are happy to investigate any positive detections for our customers and provide further information on any detections.

The results of the scan are for informational purposes only and are not intended to be used as evidence in legal proceedings. Any detection may need to be verified by a digital forensics examiner in this instance.
