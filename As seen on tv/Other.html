Safe Shopping Guarantee

We take security very seriously. Our entire site, including all credit card and personal information, is encrypted utilizing Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) server technology before being transmitted over the internet. All information that you provide during the shopping process is encrypted for your protection and held completely confidential.

We guarantee that every purchase you make will be 100% safe.

Shipping Policies

All of the items on our site are In Stock and ship within 1-2 days unless otherwise indicated. Most orders are shipped same day, excluding weekends.

Credit Card Data Storage

As Seen On TV does not store credit card data on our servers. When credit card data is entered, it's transmitted directly to the processor, and the processor returns an encrypted token to us that is used to charge your card for the transaction. This token can only be used by our server for processing transactions on our site, so if the token is ever compromised it cannot be used to make purchases on other sites.

Multi-Device Access Control

A feature of our site allows you to stay signed in from multiple devices, such as your Smartphone and tablet. You can see what devices are accessing your account at any time from the Devices page under My Account, and sign them out remotely if needed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell customer data or share it with any third parties. Most of the products on our site ship from one of our own warehouses, however some products ship directly from the manufacturer or a third party seller. In these cases, your email address, payment information, and purchase history will not be shared with them; We provide them only with your ship-to address for the purpose of fulfilling the order.

Subscription and Autoship Items

As Seen On TV does not offer any products that trigger a subscription or auto-ship program. The only time we will ever charge you is when you check out through our site, and we will always charge the exact amount shown on the Order Total field.

Hassle Free Returns

Our no-hassle return policy is pretty simple: If you don't like it, send it back within 15 days for a full refund.
