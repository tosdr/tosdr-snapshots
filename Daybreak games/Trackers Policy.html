This Cookie Policy describes the different categories of cookies and similar technologies that may be applied on the websites ("Sites") of Daybreak Game Company LLC ("Daybreak"). We may change this Cookie Policy at any time. Please take a look at the "LAST REVISED" legend at the top of this page to see when this Cookie Policy was last revised. Any changes in this Cookie Policy will become effective when we make the revised Cookie Policy available on or through the Sites.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES AND WHAT DO THEY DO?

We collect information through cookies, pixels, and other tracking technologies (collectively “Cookies”) to deliver and improve the Daybreak services, and to display relevant content, products, services, and advertising.

Cookies are a standard feature of websites that allow us to store small amounts of data on your computer about your visit to our Sites. Cookies may be used to enable the functionality and services provided by our Sites and may be used for analytics and advertising purposes as described below. A tracking pixel is a transparent graphic image (usually 1 pixel x 1 pixel or Javascript tag) that is placed on a web page and, in combination with a cookie, allows us to collect information regarding the use of the web page that contains the tracking pixel. You may choose whether to accept Cookies by changing the settings on your browser or choosing the opt out provided below. However, if you disable Cookies on our Sites, your experience on the Sites may be diminished and some features may not work as intended.
