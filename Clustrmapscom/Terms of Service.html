Description of Service


ClustrMaps is a combined hit counter, visitor tracker and geographic visualization tool. We provide some HTML that you copy into a web page or 'blog sidebar'; the HTML includes a link to a thumbnail map image generated by us; when that image loads, it increments a counter on the ClustrMaps site and, when the map is updated (daily, weekly, or monthly, depending on your traffic) it shows the locations of all the visitors to your page since the beginning of a specific time interval; clicking on that thumbnail map image reveals a larger world map display.

Cookies and third-party advertising

We do not allow third-party cookies to be used on thumbnail maps but we may use first-party cookies, i.e. cookies set by the clustrmaps.com servers, on certain thumbnail maps [only in the case of 'code option A' with JavaScript] and also our site generally, in all such cases to facilitate our own analytics, advertising or affiliate reward schemes. In such cases information is provided in an anonymized, aggregated form and does not include any personally identifiable information, for example name, address, email address, or telephone number. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, please visit http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp. Web browsers are typically set up to accept cookies by default, but you can easily reset your own browser to reject cookies or to let you know when a cookie is being sent.
