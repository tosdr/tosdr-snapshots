This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Digital Respons-Ability website.

Digital Respons-Ability secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or
disclosure. Digital Respons-Ability uses the following methods for this purpose:

-SSL Protocol

  • Comply with an existing legal obligation; or
  • Otherwise use your personal information, internally, in a lawful manner that is compatible with the context in which you provided the information.
